11-masala

Suyuqliklarda elektr toki

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Elektrolitning $0^\circ\text{C}$ dagi qarshiligi $6\ \Omega$ ga teng. Uning $50^\circ\text{C}$ dagi qarshiligini toping ($\Omega$). $\alpha=-0,002\ \text{K}^{-1}$.

A) 6,6. B) 6. C) 0. D) 3,2. E) 5,4.

Yechim

Temperaturaga bog‘liq qarshilik formulasi:

$$R=R_0(1+\alpha\Delta T)$$

Bu yerda $R_0=6\ \Omega$ va $\Delta T=50\ \text{K}$.

$$R=6\bigl(1-0,002\cdot50\bigr)=6\cdot0,9=5,4\ \Omega$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
