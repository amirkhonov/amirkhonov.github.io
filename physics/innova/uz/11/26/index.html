26-masala

Egri chiziqli harakatda tezlanish

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Avtomobil egrilik radiusi 200 m bo’lgan yo’l bo’ylab 72 km/h tezlik bilan harakatlanayotgan bo’lsa, uning markazga intilma tezlanishi $a_1$ ga teng bo’ladi. Agar avtomobilning tezligi 2 marta kamaysa tezlanish $a_2$ ga teng bo’ladi. $a_1/a_2$ ni toping.

A) 2. B) 3. C) 4. D) 5. E) 1.

Yechim

$v_1=72$ km/h $=20$ m/s, $R=200$ m.

$$a_1=\frac{v_1^2}{R}=\frac{400}{200}=2\ \text{m/s}^2$$

$v_2=v_1/2=10$ m/s:

$$a_2=\frac{v_2^2}{R}=\frac{100}{200}=0{,}5\ \text{m/s}^2$$

$$\frac{a_1}{a_2}=\frac{2}{0{,}5}=4$$

Javob: C
