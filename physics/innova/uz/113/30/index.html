30-masala

Ideal tebranishlar konturi uchun Tomson formulasi

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Tebranish konturidagi erkin tebranishlar chastotasi 50 Hz. Kondensator sig’imi 20 μF. G’altak induktivligi qanday (H)? $\pi^2=10$.

A) 0,5. B) 1. C) 2,5. D) 5. E) 10.

Yechim

Berilganlar: $f = 50$ Hz, $C = 20$ μF $= 2\cdot10^{-5}$ F.

Chastota formulasidan induktivlikni topamiz:

$$f = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}} \quad \Rightarrow \quad L = \frac{1}{4\pi^2 f^2 C}$$

$$4\pi^2 f^2 C = 4\cdot10\cdot50^2\cdot2\cdot10^{-5} = 40\cdot2500\cdot2\cdot10^{-5} = 2$$

$$L = \frac{1}{2} = 0{,}5\text{ H}$$

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
