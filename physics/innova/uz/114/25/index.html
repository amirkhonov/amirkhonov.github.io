25-masala

Tebranishlar konturida energiya

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Ideal tebranish konturida zaryadlangan kondensator g’altakka ulanganda, tebranishlar davrining qanday qismidan keyin konturdagi elektr maydon energiyasi magnit maydon energiyasidan 3 marta katta bo’ladi?

A) T/12. B) T/8. C) T/6. D) T/2. E) T/3.

Yechim

Kondensator boshlang’ich paytda to’la zaryadlangan, shu sababli:

$$q(t) = q_m\cos(\omega t), \qquad i(t) = -q_m\omega\sin(\omega t)$$

Elektr va magnit maydon energiyalari:

$$W_E = \frac{q_m^2}{2C}\cos^2(\omega t), \qquad W_B = \frac{Li_m^2}{2}\sin^2(\omega t) = \frac{q_m^2}{2C}\sin^2(\omega t)$$

(oxirgi tenglik $\omega^2 = 1/LC$ munosabatidan kelib chiqadi, bunda maksimal energiyalar teng).

Shart bo’yicha $W_E = 3W_B$:

$$\cos^2(\omega t) = 3\sin^2(\omega t) \implies \tan^2(\omega t) = \frac{1}{3} \implies \tan(\omega t) = \frac{1}{\sqrt3}$$

Bundan:

$$\omega t = \frac{\pi}{6}$$

Vaqtni davrga nisbatan ifodalaymiz ($\omega = 2\pi/T$):

$$t = \frac{\pi/6}{2\pi/T} = \frac{T}{12}$$

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
