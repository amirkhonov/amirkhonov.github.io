115-boʻlim · Elektromagnit tebranishlar va to'lqinlar

10-masala

O'zgaruvchan tok generatori

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Induksiyasi 1,2 T bo’lgan bir jinsli magnit maydonda yuzi 2,5·10⁻³ m² bo’lgan simli ramka tekis aylanganda hosil bo’ladigan induksiya EYUK ning maksimal qiymati 6 V ga teng. Ramkaning aylanish chastotasini toping (Hz).

A) 318. B) 230. C) 153. D) 300. E) 530.

Yechim

Berilgan: $B=1{,}2\text{ T}$, $S=2{,}5\cdot10^{-3}\text{ m}^2$, $\varepsilon_0=6\text{ V}$, $N=1$.

Induksion EYUK ning amplitudasi:

$$\varepsilon_0=NBS\omega$$

Bundan burchak tezlik:

$$\omega=\frac{\varepsilon_0}{BS}=\frac{6}{1{,}2\cdot2{,}5\cdot10^{-3}}=\frac{6}{0{,}003}=2000\text{ rad/s}$$

Aylanish chastotasi:

$$f=\frac{\omega}{2\pi}=\frac{2000}{6{,}283}\approx318{,}3\text{ Hz}$$

Javob: A
