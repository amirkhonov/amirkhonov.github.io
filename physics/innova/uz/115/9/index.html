9-masala

O'zgaruvchan tok generatori

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Sirt yuzi S=48 cm² bo’lgan va o’tkazgichdan yasalgan halqa induksiyasi 0,5 T bo’lgan bir jinsli magnit maydonda 200 Hz chastota bilan aylanmoqda. Agar aylanish o’qi halqa tekisligida yotsa va halqaning elektr qarshiligi R=1,6 Ω bo’lsa, unda hosil bo’luvchi induksiya tokining vaqtga bog’lanish tenglamasi qanday bo’ladi?

A) $i = 1,28\sin5574t$. B) $i = 5\sin200\pi t$. C) $i = 0,588\sin747t$. D) $i = 1,1\sin6,28t$. E) $i = 1,884\sin400\pi t$.

Yechim

Berilgan: $S=48\text{ sm}^2=0{,}0048\text{ m}^2$, $B=0{,}5\text{ T}$, $f=200\text{ Hz}$, $R=1{,}6\ \Omega$, $N=1$ (halqa).

Burchak tezlik:

$$\omega=2\pi f=2\pi\cdot200=400\pi\approx1256{,}6\text{ rad/s}$$

Induksion EYUK ning amplitudasi:

$$\varepsilon_0=NBS\omega=1\cdot0{,}5\cdot0{,}0048\cdot1256{,}6\approx3{,}016\text{ V}$$

Tokning amplituda qiymati:

$$I_0=\frac{\varepsilon_0}{R}=\frac{3{,}016}{1{,}6}\approx1{,}884\text{ A}$$

Demak, tok:

$$i=1{,}884\sin400\pi t\text{ (A)}$$

Javob: E
