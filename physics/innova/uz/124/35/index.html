124-boʻlim · Optika

35-masala

Yorug'likning sinishi

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Agar yorug’lik nuri yassi parallel shisha (n=1,7) plastinkaga 53° burchak ostida tushib, undan o’tishda 2 cm masofaga siljigan bo’lsa, plastinkaning qalinligini toping (cm). $\sin 53° = 0{,}8$.

A) 1,8. B) 2,6. C) 3,4. D) 1,6. E) 4,2.

Yechim

Berilgan: $n=1{,}7$, $\alpha=53°$, siljish $l=2$ sm. $\sin 53°=0{,}8$, demak $\cos 53°=0{,}6$.

Avval sinish burchagini topamiz:

$$\sin\gamma = \frac{\sin\alpha}{n} = \frac{0{,}8}{1{,}7} \approx 0{,}4706$$

Bundan $\gamma \approx 28{,}1°$, $\cos\gamma \approx 0{,}8823$.

Yassi parallel plastinkada yorug’likning parallel siljish (lateral offset) formulasi:

$$l = t\cdot\frac{\sin(\alpha-\gamma)}{\cos\gamma}$$

bunda t — plastinka qalinligi.

$\alpha-\gamma \approx 53° - 28{,}1° = 24{,}9°$, $\sin(24{,}9°)\approx 0{,}421$.

Bundan qalinlikni topamiz:

$$t = \frac{l\cos\gamma}{\sin(\alpha-\gamma)} = \frac{2\cdot 0{,}8823}{0{,}421} \approx 4{,}19 \text{ sm}$$

Bu taxminan 4,2 sm bo’lib, E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
