18-masala

Aylana bo'ylab notekis harakat

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Tinch turgan jism 4 m radiusli aylana bo’ylab harakatlana boshladi. U o’z harakatining dastlabki 4 s i da aylananing yarmini bosib o’tdi. Jismning 4 s dagi umumiy tezlanishini (m/s²) toping.

A) $\pi^2$. B) $\pi/2$. C) $\dfrac{\pi}{2}\sqrt{4\pi^2+1}$. D) $\sqrt{2\pi^2+3}$. E) $\sqrt{\pi^2-1}$.

Yechim

Jism tinch holatdan ($v_0=0$) tekis tezlanuvchan harakatlanadi. Radius $R=4$ m, aylananing yarmiga teng yo’l:

$$S = \frac{1}{2}\cdot2\pi R = \pi R = 4\pi \text{ m}$$

Bu yo’l $t=4$ s da bosib o’tilgan:

$$S = \frac{a_t t^2}{2} \quad \Rightarrow \quad 4\pi = \frac{a_t\cdot4^2}{2} = 8a_t \quad \Rightarrow \quad a_t = \frac{4\pi}{8} = \frac{\pi}{2} \text{ m/s}^2$$

4 s dagi tezlik:

$$v = a_t t = \frac{\pi}{2}\cdot4 = 2\pi \text{ m/s}$$

Normal tezlanish:

$$a_n = \frac{v^2}{R} = \frac{(2\pi)^2}{4} = \frac{4\pi^2}{4} = \pi^2 \text{ m/s}^2$$

Umumiy tezlanish:

$$a = \sqrt{a_t^2+a_n^2} = \sqrt{\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)^2+(\pi^2)^2} = \sqrt{\frac{\pi^2}{4}+\pi^4} = \sqrt{\frac{\pi^2}{4}(1+4\pi^2)} = \frac{\pi}{2}\sqrt{4\pi^2+1}$$

Javob: C
