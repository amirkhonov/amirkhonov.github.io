Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

8 m uzunlikdagi binoning fokus masofasi 5 cm bo‘lgan fotoapparat yordamida olingan tasviri uzunligi 0,01 m bo‘lsa, bino necha metr masofadan rasmga olingan?

A) 4. B) 40,05. C) 41. D) 13. E) 4,l.

Yechim

Kattalashtirish $k=0,01/8=1/800$. Shuning uchun $v=ku$, hamda

$$\frac1f=\frac1u+\frac1{ku},\qquad u=f\left(1+\frac1k\right)=0,05\cdot801=40,05\ \text{m}.$$

Javob: B
