20-masala

Massa va energiya orasidagi bog'lanish

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Tinchlikdagi energiyasi 2,8 TJ bo‘lgan zarra $0,6c$ tezlik bilan harakatlansa, impulsi qanchaga (kg·m/s) teng bo‘ladi? $c$ — yorug‘likning vakuumdagi tezligi.

A) 14000. B) 7000. C) 10500. D) 3500. E) 5000.

Yechim

$$p=\gamma m_0v=1,25\cdot\frac{E_0}{c^2}\cdot0,6c=0,75\frac{E_0}{c}.$$

$$p=0,75\cdot\frac{2,8\cdot10^{12}}{3\cdot10^8}=7000\ \text{kg·m/s}.$$

Javob: B
