24-masala

Kuch. Nyutonning birinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Ushbu uchta kuchning teng ta’sir etuvchisini toping.

A) F. B) 2F. C) √2F. D) 3F. E) 2√2F.

Yechim

Rasmdagi umumiy nuqtadan uchta kuch chiqadi: $2F$ — gorizontaldan 45° yuqoriga chapga, $F$ — gorizontaldan 45° yuqoriga o’ngga, $\sqrt2F$ — gorizontal chapga (asos bo’ylab).

Koordinatalar (gorizontal o’ng — musbat $x$):

$$\vec{(2F)} = (2F\cos135°;,2F\sin135°) = \left(-\sqrt2 F;,\sqrt2 F\right)$$ $$\vec F = (F\cos45°;,F\sin45°) = \left(\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}F;,\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}F\right)$$ $$\vec{(\sqrt2F)} = (-\sqrt2F;,0)$$

Yig’indi:

$$\Sigma F_x = -\sqrt2F+\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}F-\sqrt2F = -\tfrac{3\sqrt2}{2}F$$ $$\Sigma F_y = \sqrt2F+\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}F = \tfrac{3\sqrt2}{2}F$$

$$R = \sqrt{\left(\tfrac{3\sqrt2}{2}F\right)^2+\left(\tfrac{3\sqrt2}{2}F\right)^2} = \tfrac{3\sqrt2}{2}F\cdot\sqrt2 = 3F$$

Javob: D
