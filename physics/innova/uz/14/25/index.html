25-masala

Kuch. Nyutonning birinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Ushbu uchta kuchning teng ta’sir etuvchisini toping.

A) F. B) √3F. C) 2F. D) 2√3F. E) 2√2F.

Yechim

Umumiy nuqtadan uchta kuch chiqadi: $3F$ — gorizontaldan 60° yuqoriga chapga, $F$ — gorizontal o’ngga, $2F$ — gorizontaldan 60° pastga chapga.

Koordinatalar (gorizontal o’ng — musbat $x$):

$$\vec F = (F;,0)$$ $$\vec{(3F)} = (3F\cos120°;,3F\sin120°) = \left(-\tfrac32F;,\tfrac{3\sqrt3}{2}F\right)$$ $$\vec{(2F)} = (2F\cos240°;,2F\sin240°) = \left(-F;,-\sqrt3F\right)$$

Yig’indi:

$$\Sigma F_x = F-\tfrac32F-F = -\tfrac32F$$ $$\Sigma F_y = \tfrac{3\sqrt3}{2}F-\sqrt3F = \tfrac{\sqrt3}{2}F$$

$$R = \sqrt{\left(\tfrac32F\right)^2+\left(\tfrac{\sqrt3}{2}F\right)^2} = \sqrt{\tfrac94F^2+\tfrac34F^2} = \sqrt{3F^2} = \sqrt3F$$

Javob: B
