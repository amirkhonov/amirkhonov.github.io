26-masala

Kuch. Nyutonning birinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Ushbu uchta kuchning teng ta’sir etuvchisini toping(N). cos37°=0,8; cos53°=0,6.

A) 20. B) 30. C) 40. D) 50. E) 60.

Yechim

Rasmda umumiy nuqtadan uchta kuch chiqadi: $F_1=40$ N — gorizontaldan 37° yuqoriga o’ngga, $F_2=30$ N — gorizontaldan 53° pastga o’ngga, $F_3=80$ N — gorizontal chapga.

$\sin37°=0,6$, $\sin53°=0,8$ (37° va 53° — bir-birini to’ldiruvchi burchaklar).

$$\vec F_1=(F_1\cos37°;,F_1\sin37°)=(40\cdot0,8;,40\cdot0,6)=(32;,24)$$ $$\vec F_2=(F_2\cos53°;,-F_2\sin53°)=(30\cdot0,6;,-30\cdot0,8)=(18;,-24)$$ $$\vec F_3=(-80;,0)$$

Yig’indi:

$$\Sigma F_x = 32+18-80 = -30, \qquad \Sigma F_y = 24-24+0 = 0$$

$$F = \sqrt{(-30)^2+0^2} = 30 \text{ N}$$

Javob: B
