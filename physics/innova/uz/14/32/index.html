32-masala

Kuch. Nyutonning birinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Ushbu kuchlarning teng ta’sir etuvchisini toping(N).

A) √2. B) 10√2. C)15. D) 20. E) 20√2.

Yechim

Rasmda beshta kuch umumiy nuqtadan chiqadi. $F_1=15$ N va $F_2=45$ N bir to’g’ri chiziqda, qarama-qarshi yo’nalgan (tikka yuqoriga va pastga) — bularni $90°$ va $-90°$ deb olamiz.

$F_3=30$ N — $F_1$ dan 60° chapga og’gan ($150°$), $F_4=10$ N — $F_1$ dan 60° o’ngga og’gan, gorizontaldan esa 30° yuqorida ($30°$). $F_5=20$ N — gorizontaldan 30° pastda, lekin bu burchak vertikal ($F_2$) chizig’idan o’lchangan, ya’ni $F_5$ gorizontaldan $60°$ pastda ($-60°$).

$$\vec F_1=(0;15), \quad \vec F_2=(0;-45)$$ $$\vec F_3=(30\cos150°;30\sin150°)=(-15\sqrt3;15)$$ $$\vec F_4=(10\cos30°;10\sin30°)=(5\sqrt3;5)$$ $$\vec F_5=(20\cos(-60°);20\sin(-60°))=(10;-10\sqrt3)$$

Yig’indi:

$$\Sigma F_x = 0+0-15\sqrt3+5\sqrt3+10 = 10-10\sqrt3$$ $$\Sigma F_y = 15-45+15+5-10\sqrt3 = -10-10\sqrt3$$

$$F=\sqrt{(10-10\sqrt3)^2+(-10-10\sqrt3)^2}=\sqrt{800}=20\sqrt2 \text{ N}$$

Javob: E
