69-masala

Atom yadrosining tuzilishi. Radioaktivlik

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

$^{238}{92}U$ izotopi yadrosi bir necha $\alpha$- va $\beta$-yemirilishdan so’ng, $^{210}{82}Pb$ atomi yadrosiga aylandi. Bunda qancha $\alpha$- va $\beta$-yemirilish sodir bo’lgan?

A) 2-$\alpha$; 8-$\beta$. B) 5-$\alpha$; 5-$\beta$. C) 6-$\alpha$; 4-$\beta$. D) 7-$\alpha$; 4-$\beta$. E) 4-$\alpha$; 6-$\beta$.

Yechim

$$\Delta Z = 82-92=-10, \qquad \Delta A = 210-238=-28$$

$\Delta A=-28$ — 7 ta $\alpha$-yemirilish ($\Delta Z=-14$).

Qolgan farq $-10-(-14)=+4$ — 4 ta $\beta$-yemirilish beradi.

Javob: D
