22-masala

14-iyul, 1-smena

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

  1. Если на рисунке сила, действующая на частицу $+2q$, равна $2F$, определите силу, действующую на частицу $+3q$.

A) $34F/39$ B) $68F/39$ C) $68F/13$ D) $34F/26$

Yechim

Rasmda uchta zaryad bir chiziqda joylashgan: $-q$, undan $6a$ masofada $+2q$, undan yana $3a$ masofada $+3q$.

$+2q$ zaryadga ta’sir etuvchi kuchlar:

$-q$ dan (tortishish, $6a$ masofada), chapga yo’nalgan:

$$F_1 = \frac{kq\cdot2q}{(6a)^2} = \frac{2kq^2}{36a^2} = \frac{kq^2}{18a^2}$$

$+3q$ dan (itarilish, $3a$ masofada), chapga yo’nalgan:

$$F_2 = \frac{k\cdot2q\cdot3q}{(3a)^2} = \frac{6kq^2}{9a^2} = \frac{2kq^2}{3a^2}$$

Ikkala kuch ham chapga yo’nalgani uchun qo’shiladi:

$$F_{2q} = F_1+F_2 = \frac{kq^2}{18a^2}+\frac{12kq^2}{18a^2} = \frac{13kq^2}{18a^2} = 2F$$

Bundan:

$$\frac{kq^2}{a^2} = \frac{36F}{13} \equiv C$$

$+3q$ zaryadga ta’sir etuvchi kuchlar:

$-q$ dan (tortishish, $9a$ masofada), chapga ($-q$ tomon) yo’nalgan:

$$F_3 = \frac{kq\cdot3q}{(9a)^2} = \frac{3kq^2}{81a^2} = \frac{kq^2}{27a^2} = \frac{C}{27}$$

$+2q$ dan (itarilish, $3a$ masofada), o’ngga yo’nalgan:

$$F_4 = \frac{k\cdot2q\cdot3q}{(3a)^2} = \frac{2kq^2}{3a^2} = \frac{2C}{3}$$

Natijaviy kuch (o’ngni musbat deb olsak):

$$F_{3q} = F_4-F_3 = \frac{2C}{3}-\frac{C}{27} = \frac{18C-C}{27} = \frac{17C}{27} = \frac{17}{27}\cdot\frac{36F}{13} = \frac{612F}{351} = \frac{68F}{39}$$

Javob: B
