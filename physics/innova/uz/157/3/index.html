Savol

  1. (Вопрос НС) Груз массой 18 кг поднимается вверх. Найдите разность весов в интервалах A и C (Н).

Rasmdagi grafik — tezlikning vaqtga bog’liqligi $v(t)$: $t=0$ dan $t=3$ s gacha tezlik chiziqli ravishda $0$ dan $5$ m/s gacha ortadi (bu qism grafikda “A” deb belgilangan), $t=3$ s dan $t=7$ s gacha tezlik $5$ m/s da doimiy qoladi, so’ngra $t=7$ s dan $t=9$ s gacha tezlik $5$ m/s dan $0$ gacha kamayadi (bu qism “C” deb belgilangan).

A) 30 B) 15 C) 75 D) 0

Yechim

Yuk tros orqali tepaga ko’tarilmoqda, shuning uchun trosning tarangligi (ya’ni yukning “vazni”) Nyutonning ikkinchi qonuniga bo’ysunadi:

$$N - mg = ma \implies N = m(g+a)$$

bu yerda $a$ — yukning tezlanishi (yuqoriga yo’nalgan bo’lsa musbat).

A oralig’ida ($t=0$ dan $3$ s gacha): tezlik $0$ dan $5$ m/s gacha ortadi, ya’ni harakat tezlashuvchi (tezlanish yuqoriga yo’nalgan):

$$a_A = \frac{5-0}{3} = \frac{5}{3}\ \text{m/s}^2$$

$$N_A = m(g+a_A) = 18\left(10+\frac{5}{3}\right) = 18\cdot\frac{35}{3} = 210\ \text{N}$$

C oralig’ida ($t=7$ dan $9$ s gacha): tezlik $5$ m/s dan $0$ gacha kamayadi, ya’ni harakat sekinlanuvchi (tezlanish pastga yo’nalgan):

$$a_C = \frac{0-5}{2} = -2{,}5\ \text{m/s}^2$$

$$N_C = m(g+a_C) = 18(10-2{,}5) = 18\cdot7{,}5 = 135\ \text{N}$$

Vaznlar farqi:

$$\Delta N = N_A - N_C = 210 - 135 = 75\ \text{N}$$

Javob: C
