41-masala

Nyutonning ikkinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Rasmda massasi 2 kg bo’lgan jism tezligining vaqtga bog’lanish grafigi keltirilgan. Harakatning har bir bo’lagida jismga ta’sir etuvchi natijaviy kuch F qanday (N)?

A) 2; 0; −1. B) 4; 0; −2. C) 0; 10; 10. D) 0; 5; 10. E) 7,5; 10; −5.

Yechim

Grafikdan uch bo’lak ko’rinadi:

I bo’lak ($0$ dan $5$ s gacha, $v:0\to10$ m/s):

$$a_1=\frac{10}{5}=2 \text{ m/s}^2 \implies F_1=ma_1=2\cdot2=4 \text{ N}$$

II bo’lak ($5$ dan $10$ s gacha, $v=10$ m/s o’zgarmas):

$$a_2=0 \implies F_2=0$$

III bo’lak ($10$ dan $20$ s gacha, $v:10\to0$ m/s):

$$a_3=\frac{0-10}{20-10}=-1 \text{ m/s}^2 \implies F_3=ma_3=2\cdot(-1)=-2 \text{ N}$$

Javob: B
