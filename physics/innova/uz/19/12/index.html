12-masala

Og'irlik kuchi

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Merkuriy sayyorasining massasi $3\cdot10^{23}\ kg$, radiusi 2400 km. Merkuriy sayyorasi sirtidagi erkin tushish tezlanishini toping (m/s²). $G \approx \dfrac{20}{3}\cdot10^{-11} \ \dfrac{N\cdot m^2}{kg^2}$.

A) 1,6. B) 2,7. C) 4,8. D) 5,9. E) 3,5.

Yechim

$$g = \frac{GM}{R^2}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz: $M=3\cdot10^{23}\ kg$, $R=2400\ km = 2{,}4\cdot10^6\ m$, $G=\dfrac{20}{3}\cdot10^{-11}$:

$$g = \frac{\frac{20}{3}\cdot10^{-11}\cdot 3\cdot10^{23}}{(2{,}4\cdot10^6)^2} = \frac{20\cdot10^{12}}{5{,}76\cdot10^{12}} \approx 3{,}47 \ m/s^2$$

Eng yaqin javob 3,5 m/s².

Javob: E
