23-masala

To'g'ri chiziqli tekis harakat

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

23*. Yerdan 135 m balandlikdan uchib ketayotgan dronni razvetkachi durbin yordamida nazorat qilmoqda. Biror paytda dron vertikalga nisbatan $45°$ burchak ostida ko’rindi. Shundan 6 s o’tgach $53°$ burchak ostida ko’rindi.

Bu ma’lumotlardan foydalanib, dronning tezligini (km/h) aniqlang. $\sin 53° = 0{,}8$; $\cos 53° = 0{,}6$.

A) 15. B) 34. C) 48. D) 27. E) 9.

Yechim

Dron $h=135$ m balandlikda gorizontal chiziq bo’ylab uchmoqda. Kuzatuvchidan dronning tik ostidagi nuqtagacha bo’lgan gorizontal masofa $x$, vertikaldan og’ish burchagi $\alpha$ bo’lsa:

$$x = h \cdot \tan\alpha$$

Birinchi holatda ($\alpha_1=45°$):

$$x_1 = 135 \cdot \tan 45° = 135 \cdot 1 = 135 \text{ m}$$

Ikkinchi holatda ($\alpha_2=53°$):

$$\tan 53° = \frac{\sin 53°}{\cos 53°} = \frac{0{,}8}{0{,}6} = \frac{4}{3}$$

$$x_2 = 135 \cdot \frac{4}{3} = 180 \text{ m}$$

Dron $6$ s ichida bosib o’tgan gorizontal masofa:

$$\Delta x = x_2 - x_1 = 180 - 135 = 45 \text{ m}$$

Dronning tezligi:

$$v = \frac{\Delta x}{t} = \frac{45}{6} = 7{,}5 \text{ m/s}$$

km/h ga o’tkazamiz:

$$v = 7{,}5 \cdot 3{,}6 = 27 \text{ km/h}$$

Javob: D
