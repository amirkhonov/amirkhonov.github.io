33-masala

To'g'ri chiziqli tekis harakat

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

33*. Rasmda daraxt – sanoq jismi, unga bog’langan koordinatalar sistemasi va harakat boshi hamda oxiridagi soatning ko’rsatkichlari keltirilgan. Mazkur rasmga ko’ra avtomobilning x = x(t) [m] harakat tenglamasini tuzing.

Rasmda: $x$ o’qida $0$, $900$, $1800$, $2700$, $3600$ (m) belgilari. Soat $09:05$ da avtomobil $\vec{v}$ tezlik vektori bilan $x=1800$ m nuqtada, soat $09:08$ da esa $x=3600$ m nuqtada ko’rsatilgan.

A) x = 1800 + 20t. B) x = 3600 – 10t. C) x = 1800 + 10t. D) x = 1800 – 10t. E) x = 1500 – 10t.

Yechim

Harakat boshida ($09{:}05$, $t=0$) avtomobil $x_0=1800$ m koordinatada, harakat oxirida ($09{:}08$) esa $x=3600$ m koordinatada.

O’tgan vaqt:

$$\Delta t = 09{:}08 - 09{:}05 = 3 \text{ min} = 180 \text{ s}$$

Tezlik (musbat, chunki avtomobil $\vec{v}$ yo’nalishi bo’yicha, ya’ni $x$ ortish tomon harakatlanmoqda):

$$v = \frac{x - x_0}{\Delta t} = \frac{3600-1800}{180} = \frac{1800}{180} = 10 \text{ m/s}$$

Harakat tenglamasi:

$$x = x_0 + vt = 1800 + 10t$$

Javob: C
