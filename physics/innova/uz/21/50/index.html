21-boʻlim · Dinamika asoslari

50-masala

Vazn

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

$m$ massali sharcha aravachaga o’rnatilgan ustunga osilgan. Aravacha gorizontal yo’nalishda $a$ tezlanish bilan harakatlanadi. Sharcha ipi bilan vertikal orasidagi $\alpha$ burchakni va ipning $T$ taranglik kuchini toping.

A) $\alpha = arctg\dfrac{g}{a}$; $T = m\sqrt{g^2+a^2}$. B) $\alpha = arctg\dfrac{a}{g}$; $T = m\sqrt{g^2+a^2}$. C) $\alpha = arctg\dfrac{a}{g}$; $T \to \infty$. D) $a = 0$; $T = mg$. E) TJY.

Yechim

Sharchaga ip taranglik kuchi $T$ va og’irlik kuchi $mg$ ta’sir qiladi. Ip vertikaldan $\alpha$ burchakka og’ganda, tenglamalar:

Vertikal o’q bo’yicha (muvozanat, chunki vertikal tezlanish yo’q):

$$T\cos\alpha = mg$$

Gorizontal o’q bo’yicha (aravacha bilan birga $a$ tezlanish oladi):

$$T\sin\alpha = ma$$

Bu ikki tenglamani bo’lib:

$$tg\alpha = \frac{a}{g} \implies \alpha = arctg\frac{a}{g}$$

Taranglik kuchini topish uchun ikkala tenglamani kvadratga ko’tarib qo’shamiz:

$$T^2\cos^2\alpha + T^2\sin^2\alpha = (mg)^2+(ma)^2 \implies T^2 = m^2(g^2+a^2)$$

$$T = m\sqrt{g^2+a^2}$$

Javob: B
