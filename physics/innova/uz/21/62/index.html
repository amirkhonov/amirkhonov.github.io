21-boʻlim · Dinamika asoslari

62-masala

Vazn

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

1,5 t massali avtomobil egrilik radiusi 25 m bo’lgan botiq ko’prikdan 10 m/s tezlik bilan o’tmoqda. Ko’prikning o’rtasida avtomobilning og’irligini, teng ta’sir etuvchi kuchni va yuklanishni toping.

A) 12 kN; 5 kN; 1,5. B) 45 kN; 55 kN; 2,4. C) 64 kN; 18 kN; 3,5. D) 50 kN; 25 kN; 2,8. E) 21 kN; 6 kN; 1,4.

Yechim

$m=1500$ kg.

Og’irlik kuchi:

$$mg = 1500\cdot 10 = 15000 \text{ N} = 15 \text{ kN}$$

Teng ta’sir etuvchi (markazga intilma) kuch:

$$F_{nat} = \frac{mv^2}{R} = \frac{1500\cdot 100}{25} = 6000 \text{ N} = 6 \text{ kN}$$

Vazn (og’irlik):

$$P = mg+F_{nat} = 15+6 = 21 \text{ kN}$$

Yuklanish:

$$n = \frac{P}{mg} = \frac{21}{15} = 1,4$$

Javob: E
