19-masala

Gorizontga nisbatan burchak ostida otilgan jismning harakati

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Bir nuqtadan ikkita jism gorizontga burchak ostida rasmdagi kabi otildi. Rasmdagi ma’lumotlardan foydalanib, $x$ masofani toping(m).

A) 960. B) 720. C) 640. D) 520. E) 360.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra bitta jism $100$ m/s tezlik bilan gorizontga $37°$ burchak ostida, ikkinchisi esa $50$ m/s tezlik bilan (birinchisidan $16°$ ga farq qiluvchi, ya’ni $37°+16°=53°$ burchak ostida) otilgan.

Har birining uchish uzoqligi (gorizontal masofasi): $$R=\frac{2v_x v_y}{g}$$

$100$ m/s, $37°$ uchun: $v_x=80$, $v_y=60$ m/s: $$R_1=\frac{2\cdot80\cdot60}{10}=960 \text{ m}$$

$50$ m/s, $53°$ uchun: $v_x=50\cos53°=30$, $v_y=50\sin53°=40$ m/s: $$R_2=\frac{2\cdot30\cdot40}{10}=240 \text{ m}$$

Rasmda ko’rsatilgan $x$ — ikkala jismning tushish nuqtalari orasidagi masofa: $$x=R_1-R_2=960-240=720 \text{ m}$$

Javob: B
