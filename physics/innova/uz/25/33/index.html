33-masala

Gorizontga nisbatan burchak ostida otilgan jismning harakati

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Gorizontga $\alpha$ burchak ostida otilgan jismning harakati quyida tasvirlangan. $h/x$ ning qiymatini toping.

A) 1/3. B) 1/4. C) 1/8. D) 3/8. E) 2/3.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra boshlang’ich tezlik $v_0=\sqrt5,v$, traektoriyaning eng yuqori nuqtasidagi (gorizontal) tezlik esa $2v$ ga teng, ya’ni:

$$v_x=v_0\cos\alpha=2v \Rightarrow \cos\alpha=\frac{2v}{\sqrt5,v}=\frac{2}{\sqrt5}$$

Bundan $\sin\alpha=\sqrt{1-4/5}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt5}$, demak $v_y=v_0\sin\alpha=\sqrt5,v\cdot\dfrac{1}{\sqrt5}=v$.

Maksimal balandlik: $$h=\frac{v_y^2}{2g}=\frac{v^2}{2g}$$

Uchish uzoqligi: $$x=\frac{2v_xv_y}{g}=\frac{2\cdot2v\cdot v}{g}=\frac{4v^2}{g}$$

Nisbat: $$\frac{h}{x}=\frac{v^2/(2g)}{4v^2/g}=\frac{1}{8}$$

Javob: C
