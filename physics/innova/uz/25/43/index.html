25-boʻlim · Dinamika asoslari

43-masala

Gorizontga nisbatan burchak ostida otilgan jismning harakati

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Gorizontga burchak ostida rasmdagi kabi otilgan jism tepalik ustiga borib tushdi. $h_1/h_2$ nisbatni aniqlang.

A) 4/5. B) 1/3. C) 1/5. D) 1/8. E) 1/15.

Yechim

Jism yer sathidan otilib, gorizontal $3x$ masofada boshlanadigan, $x$ kengligidagi tepalik (balandligi $h_2$) ustiga, uning uzoq chetiga (yerdan $3x+x=4x$ masofada) tushadi. Traektoriyaning cho’qqisi (tepalik yuzasidan $h_1$ balandlikda) tepalikning yaqin chetiga to’g’ri keladi, ya’ni yerdan $3x$ masofada.

Cho’qqiga yetish vaqti $t_1=v_y/g$, shu vaqtdagi gorizontal masofa: $$v_x t_1=3x \quad (1)$$

Cho’qqidan tepalik chetigacha bo’lgan qo’shimcha vaqt $t_2$ da gorizontal masofa $x$ ga o’sadi: $$v_x t_2=x \Rightarrow t_2=\frac{x}{v_x}$$

Bu vaqt ichida jism cho’qqidan pastga qarab (tinch holatdan boshlab) $h_1$ ga tushadi: $$h_1=\frac{g t_2^2}{2}=\frac{g}{2}\left(\frac{x}{v_x}\right)^2 \quad (2)$$

Cho’qqining yer sathidan balandligi ($h_1+h_2$): $$h_1+h_2=\frac{v_y^2}{2g}=\frac{g}{2}\left(\frac{v_y}{g}\right)^2=\frac{g}{2}t_1^2$$

(1) dan $t_1=\dfrac{3x}{v_x}$, shuning uchun: $$h_1+h_2=\frac{g}{2}\left(\frac{3x}{v_x}\right)^2=9\cdot\frac{g}{2}\left(\frac{x}{v_x}\right)^2=9h_1$$

Bundan $h_2=8h_1$, ya’ni: $$\frac{h_1}{h_2}=\frac{1}{8}$$

Javob: D
