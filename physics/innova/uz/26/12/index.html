12-masala

Yerning sun'iy yo'ldoshlari

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Agar Yerning sun’iy yo’ldoshi Yer sirtidan 1700 km balandlikda aylana orbita bo’ylab harakatlansa, uning tezligi qanday bo’ladi? Yerning massasi $6\cdot10^{24}$ kg, radiusi 6400 km, gravitatsiya doimiysi $6,67\cdot10^{-11}$ N·m²/kg².

A) 70 km/s. B) 70 m/s. C) 7 m/s. D) 0,7 m/s. E) 7 km/s.

Yechim

Orbita radiusi:

$$r=R+h=6400+1700=8100 \text{ km}=8,1\cdot10^{6} \text{ m}$$

Aylana orbita bo’ylab tezlik:

$$v=\sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}=\sqrt{\frac{6,67\cdot10^{-11}\cdot6\cdot10^{24}}{8,1\cdot10^{6}}}=\sqrt{\frac{4,002\cdot10^{14}}{8,1\cdot10^{6}}}=\sqrt{4,94\cdot10^{7}}\approx7029 \text{ m/s}\approx7 \text{ km/s}$$

Javob: E
