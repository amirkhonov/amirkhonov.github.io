16-masala

Yerning sun'iy yo'ldoshlari

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Qandaydir sayyoraning sirtidagi erkin tushish tezlanishi 12,6 m/s², uning radiusi 8200 km. Bu sayyorada 131800 km balandlikda doiraviy orbita bo’ylab harakatlanadigan sun’iy yo’ldoshning tezligini hisoblang (km/s).

A) 1,14. B) 2,46. C) 3,85. D) 4,71. E) 5,68.

Yechim

Avval $GM$ ni sayyora sirtidagi $g$ orqali topamiz: $g=\dfrac{GM}{R^2}$, ya’ni $GM=gR^2$.

$$R=8200 \text{ km}=8,2\cdot10^{6} \text{ m}, \quad GM=12,6\cdot(8,2\cdot10^{6})^2=12,6\cdot6,724\cdot10^{13}\approx8,472\cdot10^{14}$$

Orbita radiusi:

$$r=R+h=8200+131800=140,000 \text{ km}=1,4\cdot10^{8} \text{ m}$$

Orbital tezlik:

$$v=\sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}=\sqrt{\frac{8,472\cdot10^{14}}{1,4\cdot10^{8}}}=\sqrt{6,051\cdot10^{6}}\approx2460 \text{ m/s}\approx2,46 \text{ km/s}$$

Javob: B
