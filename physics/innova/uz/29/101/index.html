101-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Qiyalik burchagi $30°$ bo’lgan qiya tekislikdan massasi 500 kg bo’lgan vagonetka trosga bog’langan holda tushirilmoqda. Vagonetka tezligi 2 m/s ga yetganda unga tormoz berildi. Tormozlanish vaqti 5 s va ishqalanish koeffitsienti 0,01 bo’lsa, trosning taranglik kuchini toping (N). $g=10 \text{ m/s}^2$, $\sin30°=0{,}5$, $\cos30°=0{,}87$.

A) 3100. B) 3000. C) 2900. D) 2800. E) 2650.

Yechim

Vagonetka tekislik bo’ylab pastga tushayotib, tormoz berilgach 2 m/s dan 0 gacha $5$ s da sekinlanadi. Sekinlanish (tormozlanish) tezlanishi moduli:

$$a = \frac{v}{t} = \frac{2}{5} = 0{,}4 \text{ m/s}^2$$

Pastga qarab yo’nalishni musbat deb olsak, vagonetka sekinlanmoqda, ya’ni tezlanish vektori tepaga yo’nalgan ($-a$). Kuchlar tenglamasi (tekislik bo’ylab):

$$mg\sin\alpha - \mu mg\cos\alpha - T = -ma$$

$$T = mg\sin\alpha - \mu mg\cos\alpha + ma$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz:

$$mg\sin\alpha = 500\cdot10\cdot0{,}5 = 2500 \text{ N}$$

$$\mu mg\cos\alpha = 0{,}01\cdot500\cdot10\cdot0{,}87 = 43{,}5 \text{ N}$$

$$ma = 500\cdot0{,}4 = 200 \text{ N}$$

$$T = 2500 - 43{,}5 + 200 = 2656{,}5 \approx 2650 \text{ N}$$

Javob: E
