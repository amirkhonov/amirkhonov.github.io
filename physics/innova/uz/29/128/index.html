128-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob d

Savol

Rasmdagi sistemaning tezlanishini ($m/s^2$) va ipning taranglik kuchini (N) aniqlang. Jism va sirt orasidagi ishqalanish koeffitsenti $0{,}2$ ga teng.

A) $2$; $40$. B) $2$; $60$. C) $4$; $60$. D) $6$; $40$. E) $5$; $60$.

Yechim

Rasmda stol ustida $m_1=5$ kg massali jism, blok orqali unga bog’langan holda $m_2=10$ kg massali yuk osilgan.

Harakat tenglamalari:

Osilgan yuk uchun: $m_2g-T=m_2a$

Stol ustidagi jism uchun: $T-\mu m_1 g=m_1a$

Qo’shib, tezlanishni topamiz:

$$a=\frac{m_2g-\mu m_1g}{m_1+m_2}=\frac{10\cdot10-0{,}2\cdot5\cdot10}{5+10}=\frac{100-10}{15}=\frac{90}{15}=6 \ \text{m/s}^2$$

Taranglik kuchi:

$$T=m_2(g-a)=10\cdot(10-6)=40 \ \text{N}$$

Javob: D
