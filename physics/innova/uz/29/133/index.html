133-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob d

Savol

Rasmdagi sistemada $F=60$ N va ishqalanish kuchi $2$ N ga teng. Ipning taranglik kuchi (N) va sistemaning tezlanishini ($m/s^2$) aniqlang.

A) $45$; $2$. B) $64$; $4$. C) $48$; $1{,}5$. D) $54$; $1$. E) $72$; $3$.

Yechim

Rasmda stol ustidagi $4$ kg massali jismga $F=60$ N kuch gorizontga $37°$ burchak ostida (blokdan uzoqlashtiruvchi yo’nalishda) ta’sir qiladi, jism blok orqali $6$ kg massali osilgan yukka bog’langan. $\sin 37°=0{,}6$, $\cos 37°=0{,}8$.

Osilgan $6$ kg yuk uchun (pastga tushadi):

$$6g-T=6a \ \Rightarrow \ 60-T=6a$$

$4$ kg massali jism uchun (blok tomon tortiladi, $F$ va ishqalanish kuchi bunga qarshilik qiladi):

$$T-F\cos37°-F_{ishq}=4a \ \Rightarrow \ T-60\cdot0{,}8-2=4a \ \Rightarrow \ T-50=4a$$

Ikkala tenglamani qo’shamiz:

$$60-50=10a \ \Rightarrow \ a=1 \ \text{m/s}^2$$

$$T=60-6\cdot1=54 \ \text{N}$$

Javob: D
