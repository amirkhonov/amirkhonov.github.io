29-boʻlim · Dinamika asoslari

139-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob b

Savol

Rasmda keltirilgan sistemadagi jismlarning tezlanishi $a=0{,}5g$ bo’lsa, $F$ kuchning modulini toping. Barcha jismlar va sirt orasidagi ishqalanish koeffitsenti $\mu=1/7$ ga teng. $g$ – erkin tushish tezlanishi.

A) $2mg$. B) $5mg$. C) $mg$. D) $4mg$. E) $3mg$.

Yechim

Rasmda uchta jism ip bilan ketma-ket bog’langan: gorizontal yerdagi $3m$ massali jism, undan ip $37°$li qiya tekislikdagi $2m$ massali jismga, u yerdan esa tepadagi gorizontal sirtdagi $m$ massali jismga ulangan, unga $F$ kuchi qo’yilgan ($\sin37°=0{,}6$, $\cos37°=0{,}8$). Barcha ipdagi taranglik kuchlarini mos ravishda $T_1$ (3m va 2m orasida) va $T_2$ (2m va m orasida) deb belgilaymiz.

Yerdagi $3m$ jism (gorizontal, ishqalanish bilan tortiladi): $$T_1-\mu\cdot3mg=3ma$$ $$T_1=3m(a+\mu g)=3m\left(\frac{g}{2}+\frac{g}{7}\right)=3mg\cdot\frac{9}{14}=\frac{27mg}{14}$$

Qiya tekislikdagi $2m$ jism (yuqoriga tortiladi, og’irlik va ishqalanish qarshilik qiladi): $$T_2-T_1-2mg\sin37°-\mu\cdot2mg\cos37°=2ma$$ $$T_2=\frac{27mg}{14}+2mg\cdot0{,}6+\frac{1}{7}\cdot2mg\cdot0{,}8+2m\cdot\frac{g}{2}$$ $$T_2=\frac{27mg}{14}+1{,}2mg+\frac{1{,}6mg}{7}+mg=\frac{27mg+16{,}8mg+3{,}2mg+14mg}{14}=\frac{61mg}{14}$$

Tepadagi $m$ jism (gorizontal, $F$ bilan tortiladi): $$F-T_2-\mu mg=ma$$ $$F=T_2+\mu mg+ma=\frac{61mg}{14}+\frac{mg}{7}+\frac{mg}{2}=\frac{61mg+2mg+7mg}{14}=\frac{70mg}{14}=5mg$$

Javob: B
