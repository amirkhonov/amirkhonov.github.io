29-boʻlim · Dinamika asoslari

145-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob e

Savol

Ikkita bir xil $m$ massali brusok qiyalik burchagi $\alpha$ bo’lgan qiya tekislikdan birgalikda sirpanib tushmoqda (rasmga q.). Brusoklar bilan qiya tekislik orasidagi sirpanish ishqalanish koeffitsientlari $k_1$ va $k_2$ ga teng ($k_1>k_2$). Brusoklar orasidagi o’zaro ta’sir kuchini toping.

A) $mg(k_1-k_2)\sin\alpha$. B) $mg\dfrac{k_1-k_2}{k_1+k_2}\cos\alpha$. C) $mg\sqrt{k_1k_2},tg,\alpha$. D) $mg\dfrac{k_1k_2}{k_1+k_2}\sin\alpha$. E) $\dfrac{mg(k_1-k_2)\cos\alpha}{2}$.

Yechim

Rasmda oldingi (pastroqdagi) brusok — 1-brusok, ishqalanish koeffitsienti $k_1$; ortidagi (yuqoridagi) brusok — 2-brusok, ishqalanish koeffitsienti $k_2$ ($k_1>k_2$ bo’lgani uchun 2-brusok kamroq ishqalanish tufayli 1-brusokni orqadan turtadi).

Ikkala brusok birga (umumiy massa $2m$) harakatlanadi, tezlanish:

$$2mg\sin\alpha-(k_1+k_2)mg\cos\alpha=2ma$$

$$a=g\sin\alpha-\frac{g\cos\alpha(k_1+k_2)}{2}$$

1-brusok ($k_1$) uchun alohida yozamiz (kontakt kuchi $N$ uni oldinga, harakat yo’nalishida turtadi):

$$mg\sin\alpha-k_1mg\cos\alpha+N=ma$$

$$N=ma-mg\sin\alpha+k_1mg\cos\alpha$$

$a$ ni qo’yamiz:

$$N=m\left[g\sin\alpha-\frac{g\cos\alpha(k_1+k_2)}{2}\right]-mg\sin\alpha+k_1mg\cos\alpha$$

$$N=-\frac{mg\cos\alpha(k_1+k_2)}{2}+k_1mg\cos\alpha=mg\cos\alpha\left(k_1-\frac{k_1+k_2}{2}\right)=mg\cos\alpha\cdot\frac{k_1-k_2}{2}$$

$$N=\frac{mg(k_1-k_2)\cos\alpha}{2}$$

Javob: E
