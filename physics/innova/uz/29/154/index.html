154-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob b

Savol

Ipga osilgan $m=1{,}5$ kg massali sharcha gorizontal tekislikda $v=2$ m/s tezlik bilan aylanmoqda. Stol ustida unga bog’langan brusok qo’zg’almasligi uchun uning massasi $M$ eng kamida qanday bo’lishi kerak (kg)? Sharchaning aylanish radiusi $R=30$ cm, brusok va stol orasidagi ishqalanish koeffitsenti $\mu=0{,}5$ ga teng. $g=10 \ m/s^2$.

A) $6$. B) $5$. C) $10$. D) $4$. E) $1$.

Yechim

Sharcha stol chetidagi kichik blok orqali stol ustidagi $M$ massali brusokka ip bilan bog’langan. Ip bo’ylab taranglik kuchi $T$ bir xil bo’lib, sharchaning konussimon mayatnik kabi aylanishini ta’minlaydi.

Sharcha uchun vertikal muvozanat: $T\cos\alpha=mg$; gorizontal (markazga intilma): $T\sin\alpha=\dfrac{mv^2}{R}$. Bulardan:

$$T=\sqrt{(mg)^2+\left(\frac{mv^2}{R}\right)^2}=m\sqrt{g^2+\left(\frac{v^2}{R}\right)^2}$$

Sonlarni qo’yamiz ($v^2/R=4/0{,}3=40/3$ m/s²):

$$T=1{,}5\sqrt{10^2+\left(\frac{40}{3}\right)^2}=1{,}5\sqrt{100+\frac{1600}{9}}=1{,}5\sqrt{\frac{900+1600}{9}}=1{,}5\sqrt{\frac{2500}{9}}=1{,}5\cdot\frac{50}{3}=25 \ \text{N}$$

Bu $T$ kuchi stol ustidagi brusokka gorizontal tortiladi. Brusok qo’zg’almasligi uchun ishqalanish kuchi shu tortishga bardosh berishi kerak:

$$\mu Mg\geq T \ \Rightarrow \ M\geq\frac{T}{\mu g}=\frac{25}{0{,}5\cdot10}=5 \ \text{kg}$$

Demak, $M$ ning eng kichik qiymati $5$ kg.

Javob: B
