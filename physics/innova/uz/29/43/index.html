43-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Gorizontal sirtdagi jismga ishqalanish kuchi ta’sir qila boshladi. Ma’lum masofada tezligi 3 marta kamaydi. Jismning tezlanishini (m/s²), dastlabki tezligini (m/s) va to’xtagunga qadar bosib o’tgan yo’lini (m) aniqlang.

A) -3; 18; 38. B) -3; 12; 54. C) -3; 18; 54. D) -3; 24; 54. E) -4; 24; 54.

Yechim

Rasmdan ko’rinadiki, jism massasi $m=2$ kg, ishqalanish kuchi $F_{ish}=6$ N, boshlang’ich tezligi $v_0$ dan $v=\dfrac{v_0}{3}$ gacha kamaygan masofa $s_1=48$ m.

Tezlanish (ishqalanish kuchidan):

$$a=-\frac{F_{ish}}{m}=-\frac{6}{2}=-3 \text{ m/s}^2$$

Tezlik 3 marta kamaygan masofani kinematika tenglamasi orqali yozamiz:

$$\left(\frac{v_0}{3}\right)^2=v_0^2-2\cdot3\cdot48$$

$$\frac{v_0^2}{9}=v_0^2-288 \quad\Rightarrow\quad 288=v_0^2\left(1-\frac{1}{9}\right)=\frac{8}{9}v_0^2$$

$$v_0^2=324 \quad\Rightarrow\quad v_0=18 \text{ m/s}$$

To’xtagunga qadar bosib o’tgan yo’l:

$$S=\frac{v_0^2}{2|a|}=\frac{324}{2\cdot3}=\frac{324}{6}=54 \text{ m}$$

Demak, $a=-3$ m/s², $v_0=18$ m/s, $S=54$ m.

Javob: C
