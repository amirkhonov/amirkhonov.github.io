47-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Poldagi 10 kg massali yukni 120 N kuch gorizontga 30° burchak ostida pastga ta’sir etib, o’zgarmas tezlik bilan siljitgan bo’lsa, pol bilan jism orasidagi ishqalanish koeffitsienti qanday?

A) 0,4. B) 0,6. C) 0,5. D) 0,7. E) 0,65.

Yechim

Berilgan: $m=10$ kg, $F=120$ N, $\alpha=30°$ ($\sin30°=0,5$, $\cos30°\approx0,87$), $g=10$ m/s².

Kuch pastga qaratilgan (poldan pastga bosuvchi tashkil etuvchi bilan) bo’lgani uchun tayanch reaksiyasi og’irlik kuchidan katta bo’ladi:

$$N=mg+F\sin\alpha=10\cdot10+120\cdot0,5=100+60=160 \text{ N}$$

Yuk o’zgarmas tezlik bilan siljigani uchun gorizontal muvozanat:

$$F\cos\alpha=F_{ish}=\mu N$$

$$\mu=\frac{F\cos\alpha}{N}=\frac{120\cdot0,87}{160}=\frac{104,4}{160}\approx0,65$$

Javob: E
