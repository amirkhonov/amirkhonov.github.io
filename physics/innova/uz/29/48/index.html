48-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Og’irligi 200 N bo’lgan jism gorizontga 60° burchak ostida yuqoriga yo’nalgan 20 N kuch ta’sirida tekis harakatlanmoqda. Agar bu kuch 45° burchak ostida ta’sir qilsa, jism qanday (m/s²) tezlanish bilan harakat qiladi?

A) 0,5. B) 1. C) 0,6. D) 0,3. E) 0,2.

Yechim

Berilgan: $P=mg=200$ N (demak $m=20$ kg, $g=10$ m/s²), $F=20$ N.

1. 60° burchakda (tekis harakat) — ishqalanish koeffitsientini topamiz:

$$N_1=mg-F\sin60°=200-20\cdot0,866=200-17,32=182,68 \text{ N}$$

Tekis harakat sharti: $F\cos60°=\mu N_1$

$$\mu=\frac{F\cos60°}{N_1}=\frac{20\cdot0,5}{182,68}=\frac{10}{182,68}\approx0,0547$$

2. 45° burchakda — tezlanishni topamiz:

$$N_2=mg-F\sin45°=200-20\cdot0,707=200-14,14=185,86 \text{ N}$$

$$F_{ish}=\mu N_2=0,0547\cdot185,86\approx10,17 \text{ N}$$

Gorizontal tashkil etuvchi: $F\cos45°=20\cdot0,707=14,14$ N

Nyutonning ikkinchi qonuni bo’yicha:

$$a=\frac{F\cos45°-F_{ish}}{m}=\frac{14,14-10,17}{20}\approx\frac{3,97}{20}\approx0,2 \text{ m/s}^2$$

Javob: E
