82-masala

Bir necha kuch ta'siridagi harakat

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

*Jismning biror $h$ balandlikdan $t_1$ vaqtda erkin tushdi. Shu jism $h$ balandlikka ega bo’lgan $\alpha$ burchakli qiya tekislik bo’ylab $t_2$ vaqtda sirpanib tushdi. $t_1/t_2 = \sin\alpha$ bo’lsa, qiya tekislikdagi ishqalanish koeffitsienti qanday?

A) 0. B) 0,1. C) 0,15. D) 0,3. E) 0,729.

Yechim

Erkin tushish: $h = \dfrac{gt_1^2}{2} \Rightarrow t_1 = \sqrt{\dfrac{2h}{g}}$

Qiya tekislikda sirpanish: tekislik uzunligi $l = \dfrac{h}{\sin\alpha}$, tezlanish $a = g(\sin\alpha - \mu\cos\alpha)$.

$$l = \frac{at_2^2}{2} \Rightarrow t_2 = \sqrt{\frac{2l}{a}} = \sqrt{\frac{2h}{g(\sin\alpha-\mu\cos\alpha)\sin\alpha}}$$

Nisbatni topamiz:

$$\frac{t_1}{t_2} = \sqrt{\frac{2h/g}{\dfrac{2h}{g(\sin\alpha-\mu\cos\alpha)\sin\alpha}}} = \sqrt{(\sin\alpha-\mu\cos\alpha)\sin\alpha}$$

Shartga ko’ra $\dfrac{t_1}{t_2} = \sin\alpha$, ya’ni:

$$\sqrt{(\sin\alpha-\mu\cos\alpha)\sin\alpha} = \sin\alpha$$

Ikkala tomonni kvadratga oshiramiz:

$$(\sin\alpha - \mu\cos\alpha)\sin\alpha = \sin^2\alpha$$

$$\sin\alpha - \mu\cos\alpha = \sin\alpha ;\Rightarrow; \mu\cos\alpha = 0 ;\Rightarrow; \mu = 0$$

Demak, berilgan shart faqat ishqalanish bo’lmagan holda ($\mu=0$) bajariladi.

Javob: A
