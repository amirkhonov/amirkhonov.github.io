16-masala

To'g'ri chiziqli tekis harakatni grafik ravishda tasvirlash

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Ushbu rasmda jism koordinatasining vaqtga bog’lanish grafigi tasvirlangan. Jismning (1–4) s vaqt oralig’idagi ko’chishini va yo’lini aniqlang (m).

A) 1; 4. B) 2; 4. C) 1; 3. D) 2; 3. E) 1; 5.

Yechim

Grafikdan katakchalar bo’yicha koordinatalarni o’qiymiz: $x(0)=1$ m, $x(3)=4$ m (eng yuqori nuqta), $x(4)=3$ m. Chiziq $(0;1)$ dan $(3;4)$ gacha bir xil qiyalik bilan o’sadi, shuning uchun:

$$v_1 = \frac{4-1}{3-0} = 1 \text{ m/s} \quad\Rightarrow\quad x(1) = 1 + 1\cdot 1 = 2 \text{ m}$$

Demak, $x(1)=2$ m, $x(3)=4$ m, $x(4)=3$ m.

Ko’chish (1-4 s):

$$\Delta x = x(4) - x(1) = 3 - 2 = 1 \text{ m}$$

Yo’l (1-4 s): 1-3 s oralig’ida $x$ ortadi ($2\to4$), 3-4 s oralig’ida $x$ kamayadi ($4\to3$):

$$S = |x(3)-x(1)| + |x(4)-x(3)| = |4-2| + |3-4| = 2 + 1 = 3 \text{ m}$$

Shunday qilib, ko’chish $1$ m, yo’l esa $3$ m ga teng.

Eslatma: kitobdagi javob — B (2; 4), biroq grafikdagi koordinatalarni ($x(1)=2$, $x(3)=4$, $x(4)=3$) hisobga olgan holda hisob-kitoblar ko’chish $1$ m, yo’l $3$ m ekanini, ya’ni C javobini tasdiqlaydi.

Javob: C
