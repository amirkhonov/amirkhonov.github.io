Muvozanat shartlari

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Og’irligi P=30 N bo’lgan yuk rasmdagi kabi iplarga osilgan. Agar $T_1=T_2$ bo’lsa, gorizontal ipning taranglik kuchi T ni aniqlang (N).

A) 42. B) 30. C) 28. D) 24. E) 20.

Yechim

Tugunda to’rtta kuch ta’sir qiladi: gorizontal ip tarangligi $T$ (chapga, devor tomon), $T_1$ (shiftga, gorizontal bilan 53° burchak ostida), $T_2$ (o’ng devorga, o’sha devor bilan 53°, ya’ni gorizontal bilan 37° burchak ostida) va yuk og’irligi $P$ (pastga).

$\sin53°=0,8$, $\cos53°=0,6$, $\sin37°=0,6$, $\cos37°=0,8$.

Vertikal muvozanat ($T_1=T_2=T’$): $$T’\sin53° + T’\sin37° = P \implies T’(0,8+0,6)=30 \implies T’=\frac{30}{1,4}\approx21,43 \text{ N}$$

Gorizontal muvozanat: $$T = T’\cos53° + T’\cos37° = T’(0,6+0,8) = T’\cdot1,4$$

$$T = 21,43\cdot1,4 = 30 \text{ N}$$

Javob: B
