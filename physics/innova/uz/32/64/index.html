64-masala

Kuch va impuls

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Massasi 40 g bo’lgan koptok devor sirtiga nisbatan $30°$ burchak ostida urildi va tezligining qiymatini o’zgartirmay qaytdi. Zarbning o’rtacha kuchi 4 N, ta’sir vaqti 0,04 s bo’lsa, tezlikning moduli qanday (m/s)?

A) 8. B) 6. C) 5. D) 4. E) 2.

Yechim

Impuls: $I=F\Delta t = 4\cdot0,04=0,16$ N·s.

Bu impuls sirtga tik tashkil etuvchining o’zgarishiga teng:

$$I = 2mv\sin30° \Rightarrow v = \frac{I}{2m\sin30°} = \frac{0,16}{2\cdot0,04\cdot0,5} = \frac{0,16}{0,04} = 4 \text{ m/s}$$

Javob: D
