26-masala

Impulsning saqlanish qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

𝑣₁ tezlik bilan harakatlanayotgan 𝑚₁ massali aravachaga qarshi tomondan yerga nisbatan 𝑣₂ tezlik bilan harakatlanayotgan 𝑚₂ massali jism kelib tushdi. Aravacha to’xtab qolishi uchun jism unga qanday α burchak ostida tushishi kerak? ($m_2v_2 > m_1v_1$)

A) $\alpha = \arccos\dfrac{m_2v_2}{m_1v_1}$. B) $\alpha = \operatorname{arctg}\dfrac{m_1v_1}{m_2v_2}$. C) $\alpha = \arccos\dfrac{m_1v_1}{m_2v_2}$. D) $\alpha = \dfrac{m_1v_1}{m_2v_2}$. E) aravachaning to’xtashi α burchakka bog’liq emas.

Yechim

Aravacha to’xtashi uchun gorizontal yo’nalishdagi umumiy impuls nolga teng bo’lishi kerak. Jism $v_2$ tezlik bilan aravachaga $\alpha$ burchak ostida (harakat yo’nalishiga nisbatan) tushayotgani uchun uning gorizontal tashkil etuvchisi $v_2\cos\alpha$ bo’lib, aravachaning impulsiga qarshi yo’nalgan:

$$m_1v_1 = m_2v_2\cos\alpha$$

$$\cos\alpha = \frac{m_1v_1}{m_2v_2} \implies \alpha = \arccos\frac{m_1v_1}{m_2v_2}$$

Javob: C
