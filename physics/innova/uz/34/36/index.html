36-masala

Mexanik ish

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Dvigatelning: avtomobil joyidan qo’zg’alib, 27 km/h tezlikka erishguncha bajargan ishi A₁ va tezlikni 27 dan 54 km/h gacha oshirganda bajargan ishi A₂ orasida qanday munosabat o’rinli bo’ladi?

A) A₂=3A₁. B) A₂=2A₁. C) A₁=A₂. D) A₁=2A₂. E) A₁=3A₂.

Yechim

$v_1 = 27$ km/h, $v_2 = 54$ km/h $= 2v_1$.

Joyidan qo’zg’alib $v_1$ ga erishguncha:

$$A_1 = \frac{mv_1^2}{2}$$

$v_1$ dan $v_2=2v_1$ gacha oshirishda (ish-energiya teoremasi bo’yicha):

$$A_2 = \frac{mv_2^2}{2} - \frac{mv_1^2}{2} = \frac{m(2v_1)^2}{2} - \frac{mv_1^2}{2} = \frac{4mv_1^2}{2} - \frac{mv_1^2}{2} = \frac{3mv_1^2}{2} = 3A_1$$

Javob: A
