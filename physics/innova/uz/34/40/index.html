40-masala

Mexanik ish

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Rasmda avtomobil tortish kuchining o’zgarish grafigi tasvirlangan. Bu o’zgaruvchan kuchning 20 m masofada bajargan ishi qanday (J)?

A) 1,25·10⁵. B) 2·10⁴. C) 2,5·10⁵. D) 5·10⁵. E) 4·10⁵.

Yechim

Grafikda kuch $s=0$ dan $s=10$ m gacha $2\cdot10^4$ N dan $10^4$ N gacha chiziqli kamayadi, so’ng $s=10$ dan $s=20$ m gacha $10^4$ N da o’zgarmas qoladi.

Bajarilgan ish — grafik ostidagi umumiy yuza. Uni ikki qismga bo’lib hisoblaymiz:

Trapetsiya ($0$ dan $10$ m gacha):

$$A_1 = \frac{F(0)+F(10)}{2}\cdot 10 = \frac{2\cdot10^4+10^4}{2}\cdot 10 = 1,5\cdot10^4\cdot 10 = 1,5\cdot10^5 \text{ J}$$

To’g’ri to’rtburchak ($10$ dan $20$ m gacha):

$$A_2 = 10^4\cdot 10 = 10^5 \text{ J}$$

Umumiy ish:

$$A = A_1+A_2 = 1,5\cdot10^5+10^5 = 2,5\cdot10^5 \text{ J}$$

Javob: C
