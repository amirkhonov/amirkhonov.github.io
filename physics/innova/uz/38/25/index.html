25-masala

To'liq mexanik energiyaning saqlanish qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Yuqoriga tik otilgan jismning $h=15$ m balandlikdagi kinetik energiyasi shu balandlikdagi potensial energiyasining uchdan bir qismiga teng. Jism qanday boshlang’ich tezlik bilan otilgan?

A) 15. B) 10. C) 20. D) 50. E) 40.

Yechim

Berilgan: $h = 15$ m, $E_k = E_p/3$, $g = 10$ m/s².

To’liq mexanik energiya:

$$E = E_k + E_p = \frac{E_p}{3} + E_p = \frac{4}{3}E_p$$

Bundan:

$$E_p = \frac{3}{4}E = \frac{3}{4}mgH \quad \text{va} \quad E_p = mgh$$

Tenglashtiramiz:

$$mgh = \frac{3}{4}mgH \quad \Rightarrow \quad H = \frac{4h}{3} = \frac{4 \cdot 15}{3} = 20 \text{ m}$$

Boshlang’ich tezlikni topamiz ($H = v_0^2/2g$):

$$v_0 = \sqrt{2gH} = \sqrt{2 \cdot 10 \cdot 20} = \sqrt{400} = 20 \text{ m/s}$$

Javob: C
