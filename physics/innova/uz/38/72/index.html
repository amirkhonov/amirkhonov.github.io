38-boʻlim · Statika

72-masala

To'liq mexanik energiyaning saqlanish qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

1 kg massali jism bikrligi 200 N/m prujinaga 60 cm balandlikdan tushib prujinani necha cm ga siqadi?

A) 10. B) 15. C) 20. D) 5. E) 30.

Yechim

Berilgan: $m = 1$ kg, $k = 200$ N/m, $h = 60$ cm $= 0,6$ m, $g = 10$ m/s².

Energiyaning saqlanishi (jism prujinagacha $h$, so’ng prujinani $x$ ga siqib tushadi):

$$mg(h+x) = \frac{kx^2}{2}$$

$$1 \cdot 10 \cdot (0,6+x) = \frac{200x^2}{2} = 100x^2$$

$$6 + 10x = 100x^2$$

$$100x^2 - 10x - 6 = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad 50x^2 - 5x - 3 = 0$$

Kvadrat tenglama yechimi:

$$x = \frac{5+\sqrt{25+600}}{100} = \frac{5+\sqrt{625}}{100} = \frac{5+25}{100} = 0,3 \text{ m} = 30 \text{ cm}$$

Javob: E
