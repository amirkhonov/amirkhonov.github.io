Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Jism harakat tezligining 2 ta tashkil etuvchisi bir-biriga nisbatan 60° burchak ostida yo’nalgan bo’lib, modullari mos ravishda 4 va 6 m/s ga teng. Natijaviy tezlik qanday (m/s)?

A) 5. B) 7,7. C) 8,7. D) 10. E) 12,8.

Yechim

$v_1=4$ m/s, $v_2=6$ m/s, $\theta=60°$, $\cos60°=0,5$:

$$v=\sqrt{v_1^2+v_2^2+2v_1v_2\cos\theta}=\sqrt{16+36+2\cdot4\cdot6\cdot0,5}=\sqrt{52+24}=\sqrt{76}\approx8,7 \text{ m/s}$$

Javob: C
