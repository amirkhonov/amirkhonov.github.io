40-boʻlim · Statika

30-masala

Quvvat

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Massasi $m$ bo’lgan avtobus $v$ tezlik bilan qiyalik burchagi $\alpha$ bo’lgan tepalikka chiqyapti. Dvigatelning quvvati $N$ ga teng. Qarshilik koeffitsienti qanday?

A) $\dfrac{N-mg\sin\alpha}{mgv\cos\alpha}$. B) $\dfrac{Nv-mg\sin\alpha}{mgv\cos\alpha}$. C) $\dfrac{N-mg\sin\alpha}{mg\cos\alpha}$. D) $\dfrac{N-mgv\sin\alpha}{mgv\cos\alpha}$. E) TJY.

Yechim

Avtobus qiyalikda $v$ tezlik bilan tekis harakatlanmoqda, dvigatel quvvati $N$.

Tortish kuchi:

$$F = \frac{N}{v}$$

Qiyalik bo’ylab harakatga qarshi ikkita kuch ta’sir qiladi: og’irlik kuchining qiyalikka parallel tashkil etuvchisi $mg\sin\alpha$ va ishqalanish (qarshilik) kuchi $F_{ishq} = \mu mg\cos\alpha$ (normal bosim kuchi $mg\cos\alpha$ ga teng).

Tekis harakatda kuchlar muvozanatda:

$$F = mg\sin\alpha + \mu mg\cos\alpha$$

Bundan:

$$\mu mg\cos\alpha = F - mg\sin\alpha = \frac{N}{v} - mg\sin\alpha$$

Har ikki tomonni $v$ ga ko’paytiramiz:

$$\mu mgv\cos\alpha = N - mgv\sin\alpha$$

Qarshilik koeffitsientini topamiz:

$$\mu = \frac{N - mgv\sin\alpha}{mgv\cos\alpha}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
