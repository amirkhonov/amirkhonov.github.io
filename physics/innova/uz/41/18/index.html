41-boʻlim · Statika

18-masala

Foydali ish koeffitsenti

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Quyidagi ifodalarning qaysilari qiya tekislikning foydali ish koeffitsientini ifodalaydi?

  1. $\dfrac{1}{1-\mu,\mathrm{ctg},\alpha}$; 2) $\dfrac{\mathrm{tg},\alpha}{\mu+\mathrm{tg},\alpha}$; 3) $\dfrac{1}{1+\mu,\mathrm{ctg},\alpha}$; 4) $\dfrac{1}{1-\mu,\mathrm{tg},\alpha}$; 5) $\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{\sin\alpha+\mu\cos\alpha}$.

A) 1, 3, 4. B) 2, 4, 5. C) 3, 4, 5. D) 1, 3, 5. E) 2, 3, 5.

Yechim

Ishqalanishli qiya tekislikda tepaga tortib chiqarishda foydali ish $mgh$, bajarilgan (to’liq) ish esa $(mg\sin\alpha + \mu mg\cos\alpha)\cdot l$, bunda $h = l\sin\alpha$.

FIK:

$$\eta = \frac{\sin\alpha}{\sin\alpha + \mu\cos\alpha} \quad \text{(ifoda 5)}$$

Suratni va maxrajni $\cos\alpha$ ga bo’lsak:

$$\eta = \frac{\mathrm{tg},\alpha}{\mathrm{tg},\alpha + \mu} \quad \text{(ifoda 2)}$$

Suratni va maxrajni $\sin\alpha$ ga bo’lsak:

$$\eta = \frac{1}{1 + \mu,\mathrm{ctg},\alpha} \quad \text{(ifoda 3)}$$

Demak, 2, 3 va 5-ifodalar bir xil to’g’ri formulaning turli ko’rinishlari, 1- va 4-ifodalar esa noto’g’ri (ishoralari xato).

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
