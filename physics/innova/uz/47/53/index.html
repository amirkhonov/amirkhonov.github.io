53-masala

Arximed kuchi

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Suvdagi yukni ko’tarayotgan arqonning taranglik kuchi yuk hajmining yarmi suv yuzasiga chiqqanda 1,5 marta ortgan bo’lsa, yukning zichligi necha kg/m³?

A) 3000. B) 2500. C) 1500. D) 2000. E) 2250.

Yechim

Yukning massasi $m$, hajmi $V$, zichligi $\rho_j = m/V$. Suvning zichligi $\rho_s = 1000$ kg/m³.

To’liq botgan holatda arqon taranglik kuchi (suvdagi og’irlik):

$$T_1 = mg - \rho_s V g$$

Yukning yarmi suv yuzasiga chiqqanda, botgan qism hajmi $V/2$ ga teng bo’ladi, taranglik kuchi:

$$T_2 = mg - \rho_s \frac{V}{2} g$$

Shartga ko’ra $T_2 = 1,5 T_1$:

$$mg - \rho_s \frac{V}{2} g = 1,5\left(mg - \rho_s V g\right)$$

$$mg - \frac{\rho_s V g}{2} = 1,5mg - 1,5\rho_s V g$$

Hadlarni guruhlaymiz:

$$1,5\rho_s V g - \frac{\rho_s V g}{2} = 1,5mg - mg$$

$$\rho_s V g = 0,5 mg$$

Bundan:

$$\rho_s V = 0,5 m \implies \frac{m}{V} = 2\rho_s$$

Yukning zichligini topamiz:

$$\rho_j = 2 \cdot 1000 = 2000 \text{ kg/m}^3$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
