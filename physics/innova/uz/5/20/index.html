5-boʻlim · Kinematika

20-masala

Notekis harakat

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Poezd yo’lning birinchi yarmini ikkinchi yarmiga qaraganda 2 marta katta tezlik bilan bosib o’tdi. Uning butun yo’l davomidagi o’rtacha tezligi 54 km/h bo’lsa, yo’lning birinchi yarmidagi tezligi qanday (m/s) bo’lgan?

A) 20. B) 22,5. C) 22. D) 21. E) 20,5.

Yechim

$v_1=2v_2$ deb belgilaymiz.

$$v_{o’r}=\frac{2v_1v_2}{v_1+v_2}=\frac{2\times 2v_2\times v_2}{3v_2}=\frac{4v_2^2}{3v_2}=\frac{4v_2}{3}$$

Shartga ko’ra $v_{o’r}=54\ km/h$: $$\frac{4v_2}{3}=54 \Rightarrow v_2=40{,}5\ km/h$$ $$v_1=2v_2=81\ km/h$$

$v_1$ ni m/s ga o’tkazamiz: $$v_1=\frac{81}{3{,}6}=22{,}5\ m/s$$

Javob: B
