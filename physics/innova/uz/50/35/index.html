50-boʻlim · Tebranish va to'lqinlar

35-masala

Prujinali mayatnik

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

1-rasmdagi sistemaning tebranish davri 10 s, 2-rasmda esa 8 s. 3-rasmdagi yukning tebranish davrini toping (s). Barcha prujinalarning bikirligi bir xil.

A) 4. B) 5. C) 6. D) 9. E) 12.

Yechim

Rasmlarga qaraymiz: 1-rasmda bitta prujinaga $m_1$ va uning ostiga ulangan $m_2$ osilgan (jami massa $m_1+m_2$); 2-rasmda esa xuddi shunday prujinaga faqat $m_1$ osilgan; 3-rasmda esa xuddi shu prujinaga faqat $m_2$ osilgan (gorizontal holatda). Barcha prujinalarning bikrligi $k$ bir xil.

1-rasm uchun:

$$T_1^2 = 4\pi^2 \frac{m_1+m_2}{k} = 10^2 = 100$$

2-rasm uchun:

$$T_2^2 = 4\pi^2 \frac{m_1}{k} = 8^2 = 64$$

Ikkalasini ayirsak:

$$T_1^2 - T_2^2 = 4\pi^2\frac{m_2}{k} = 100 - 64 = 36$$

Bu ayni 3-rasmdagi yuk ($m_2$) uchun davrning kvadrati:

$$T_3^2 = 4\pi^2\frac{m_2}{k} = 36 \implies T_3 = 6 \text{ s}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
