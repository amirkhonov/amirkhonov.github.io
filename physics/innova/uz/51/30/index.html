30-masala

Matematik mayatnik

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Matematik mayatnik uzunligi 8 cm ortganda, tebranishlar davri 0,1 s oshdi. Tebranishlarning dastlabki davri qanday (s). (π²≈g).

A) 1,4. B) 1,6. C) 1,7. D) 2. E) 1,55.

Yechim

$\pi^2 \approx g$ shartidan tebranish davri formulasi soddalashadi:

$$T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}} = 2\pi \cdot \frac{\sqrt{l}}{\pi} = 2\sqrt{l}$$

Boshlang’ich uzunlik $l$ bo’lsin, unda $T_1 = 2\sqrt{l}$, uzunlik $\Delta l = 0{,}08$ m ortganda $T_2 = 2\sqrt{l+0{,}08}$.

Shart bo’yicha $T_2 - T_1 = 0{,}1$ s:

$$2\sqrt{l+0{,}08} - 2\sqrt{l} = 0{,}1 \Rightarrow \sqrt{l+0{,}08} - \sqrt{l} = 0{,}05$$

$x=\sqrt{l}$ deb belgilaymiz:

$$\sqrt{x^2+0{,}08} = x+0{,}05$$

Ikkala tomonni kvadratga ko’taramiz:

$$x^2+0{,}08 = x^2+0{,}1x+0{,}0025 \Rightarrow 0{,}0775 = 0{,}1x \Rightarrow x = 0{,}775$$

Demak, $l = x^2 = 0{,}600625$ m, va:

$$T_1 = 2\sqrt{l} = 2 \cdot 0{,}775 = 1{,}55 \text{ s}$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
