37-masala

Modda tuzilishi va uning asosiy xossalari

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Azot molekulasining idish devori sirtiga nisbatan 30° burchak ostida mutlaq elastik urilishi natijasida impulsning o’zgarishi 1,4∙10⁻²³ kg∙m/s ga teng bo’ldi. Azot molekulasining tezligini toping (m/s). N_A=6∙10²³ mol⁻¹. μ=28 g/mol.

A) 300. B) 600. C) 1000. D) 1200. E) 2000.

Yechim

Berilgan: $\Delta p = 1{,}4\cdot10^{-23}$ kg·m/s, $\alpha = 30°$, $\mu = 28$ g/mol $= 0{,}028$ kg/mol, $N_A = 6\cdot10^{23} \text{ mol}^{-1}$.

Bitta molekula massasi:

$$m_0 = \frac{\mu}{N_A} = \frac{0{,}028}{6\cdot10^{23}} \approx 4{,}67\cdot10^{-26} \text{ kg}$$

Elastik urilishda:

$$\Delta p = 2m_0 v\sin\alpha$$

Bundan tezlik:

$$v = \frac{\Delta p}{2m_0\sin\alpha}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz:

$$v = \frac{1{,}4\cdot10^{-23}}{2\cdot4{,}67\cdot10^{-26}\cdot0{,}5} = \frac{1{,}4\cdot10^{-23}}{4{,}67\cdot10^{-26}} \approx 300 \text{ m/s}$$

Javob: A
