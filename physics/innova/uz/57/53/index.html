53-masala

Modda tuzilishi va uning asosiy xossalari

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Sirt yuzasi 20 cm² bo’lgan qurilmaning sirtiga 1 μm qalinlikda oltin qatlami qoplandi. Qoplamda nechta oltin atomi bor? Avogadro doimiysi N_A=6,02∙10²³ mol⁻¹, oltinning atom massasi 197 m.a.b., zichligi esa 19,3 g/cm³.

A) 1,18∙10²⁰. B) 1,5∙10²⁰. C) 2∙10²⁰. D) 1,13∙10²⁰. E) 2,2∙10²⁰.

Yechim

Berilgan: $S = 20 \text{ cm}^2$, $h = 1 \text{ μm} = 10^{-4} \text{ cm}$, $\rho = 19{,}3 \text{ g/cm}^3$, $\mu = 197$ g/mol.

Qatlam hajmi:

$$V = Sh = 20\cdot10^{-4} = 2\cdot10^{-3} \text{ cm}^3$$

Massa:

$$m = \rho V = 19{,}3 \cdot 2\cdot10^{-3} \approx 0{,}0386 \text{ g}$$

Modda miqdori:

$$\nu = \frac{m}{\mu} = \frac{0{,}0386}{197} \approx 1{,}96\cdot10^{-4} \text{ mol}$$

Atomlar soni:

$$N = \nu N_A \approx 1{,}96\cdot10^{-4} \cdot 6{,}02\cdot10^{23} \approx 1{,}18\cdot10^{20}$$

Javob: A
