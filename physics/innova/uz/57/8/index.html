8-masala

Modda tuzilishi va uning asosiy xossalari

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Misning nisbiy atom massasi 64 m.a.b. Bitta mis atomining massasini toping(kg). 1 m.a.b = 1,67∙10⁻²⁷ kg.

A) 1,2∙10⁻²⁶. B) 1,5∙10⁻²⁶. C) 2,03∙10⁻²⁵. D) 1,07∙10⁻²⁵. E) 3,2∙10⁻²⁵.

Yechim

Berilgan: $A_r = 64$ m.a.b, $1$ m.a.b $= 1{,}67\cdot10^{-27}$ kg.

Bitta atom massasi:

$$m_0 = A_r \cdot 1 \text{ m.a.b.}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz:

$$m_0 = 64 \cdot 1{,}67\cdot10^{-27} = 106{,}9\cdot10^{-27} \approx 1{,}07\cdot10^{-25} \text{ kg}$$

Javob: D
