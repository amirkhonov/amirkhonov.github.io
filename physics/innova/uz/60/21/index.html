21-masala

Gaz molekulalarining o'rtacha kvadratik tezligi

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Qanday temperaturadagi kislorod molekulalarining o’rtacha kvadratik tezligi 100 K temperaturadagi vodorod molekulalarining o’rtacha kvadratik tezligiga teng bo’ladi (K)?

A) 160. B) 320. C) 800. D) 1600. E) 3200.

Yechim

Berilgan: $T_{H_2} = 100$ K, $M_{H_2} = 2$ g/mol, $M_{O_2} = 32$ g/mol.

Tezliklar tengligi shartidan:

$$\sqrt{\dfrac{3RT_{O_2}}{M_{O_2}}} = \sqrt{\dfrac{3RT_{H_2}}{M_{H_2}}} \implies \dfrac{T_{O_2}}{M_{O_2}} = \dfrac{T_{H_2}}{M_{H_2}}$$

Bundan:

$$T_{O_2} = T_{H_2} \cdot \dfrac{M_{O_2}}{M_{H_2}} = 100 \cdot \dfrac{32}{2} = 1600 \text{ K}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
