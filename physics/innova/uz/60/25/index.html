25-masala

Gaz molekulalarining o'rtacha kvadratik tezligi

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Ideal gaz temperaturasi $150°C$ ga oshirilganda, molekulalarning o’rtacha kvadratik tezligi 250 dan 500 m/s gacha ortdi. Bu tezlikni 500 m/s dan 750 m/s gacha oshirish uchun gaz temperaturasini necha gradusga ko’tarish kerak?

A) 100. B) 150. C) 250. D) 350. E) 50.

Yechim

Berilgan: $v_1 = 250$ m/s, $v_2 = 500$ m/s, $\Delta T_{12} = 150$ K (temperatura o’zgarishi Kelvin va Selsiyda bir xil).

Tezlik va absolut temperatura bog’lanishi: $v^2 \sim T$, shuning uchun:

$$\dfrac{v_2^2}{v_1^2} = \dfrac{T_2}{T_1}$$

Bundan boshlang’ich temperaturani topamiz. $T_2 = T_1 + 150$ bo’lgani uchun:

$$\dfrac{500^2}{250^2} = \dfrac{T_1 + 150}{T_1} \implies 4 = \dfrac{T_1+150}{T_1} \implies 4T_1 = T_1 + 150 \implies T_1 = 50 \text{ K}$$

Demak, $T_1 = 50$ K, $T_2 = 200$ K (bu $v = 500$ m/s ga mos keladi).

Endi $v_3 = 750$ m/s ga mos temperaturani topamiz:

$$\dfrac{v_3^2}{v_2^2} = \dfrac{T_3}{T_2} \implies \dfrac{750^2}{500^2} = \dfrac{T_3}{200} \implies 2,25 = \dfrac{T_3}{200} \implies T_3 = 450 \text{ K}$$

Kerakli temperatura oshishi:

$$\Delta T_{23} = T_3 - T_2 = 450 - 200 = 250 \text{ K}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
