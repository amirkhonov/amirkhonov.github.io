6-masala

Ideal gaz holatining tenglamasi

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Hajmi 8,31 $l$ bo’lgan idishda 140 g molekular azot 3,5 MPa bosim ostida bo’lsa, uning temperaturasi qanday (K)? R=8,31 J/(mol·K). $\mu=28\cdot10^{-3}$ kg/mol.

A) 70. B) 140. C) 280. D) 350. E) 700.

Yechim

Berilganlar: $V=8,31\cdot10^{-3}$ m³, $m=0,14$ kg, $p=3,5\cdot10^6$ Pa, $\mu=28\cdot10^{-3}$ kg/mol.

$$T = \frac{pV\mu}{mR}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz:

$$T = \frac{3,5\cdot10^6\cdot 8,31\cdot10^{-3}\cdot 28\cdot10^{-3}}{0,14\cdot 8,31} = \frac{814,38}{1,1634} \approx 700 \text{ K}$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
