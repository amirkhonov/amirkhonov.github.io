18-masala

Klapeyron tenglamasi

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

27°C da 6 m³ gaz bosimi 1 N/cm² bo’lsa, shu gazning hajmi 2 m³, temperaturasi 87°C bo’lganda, bosimi qanday (kPa) bo’ladi?

A) 20. B) 22. C) 25. D) 32. E) 36.

Yechim

Berilgan: $T_1 = 27+273=300$ K, $V_1 = 6$ m³, $p_1 = 1$ N/sm², $T_2 = 87+273=360$ K, $V_2 = 2$ m³.

Bosimni SI birligiga o’tkazamiz:

$$p_1 = 1 \ \text{N/sm}^2 = \frac{1\ \text{N}}{10^{-4}\ \text{m}^2} = 10^4 \ \text{Pa} = 10 \text{ kPa}$$

Klapeyron tenglamasiga ko’ra:

$$\frac{p_1V_1}{T_1} = \frac{p_2V_2}{T_2}$$

Bundan:

$$p_2 = \frac{p_1V_1T_2}{T_1V_2} = \frac{10\cdot 6\cdot 360}{300\cdot 2} = \frac{21600}{600} = 36 \text{ kPa}$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
