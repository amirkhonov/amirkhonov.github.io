Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

O’zgarmas massali ideal gaz 3 holati rasmda tasvirlangan 1-2-3-4 sikl bo’yicha o’zgaradi. 1-holatda gazning harorati $T_0$ ga teng. Gazning 2-, 3- va 4-holatlardagi haroratini aniqlang.

A) $T_2=3\cdot T_0$, $T_3=6\cdot T_0$, $T_4=2\cdot T_0$. B) $T_2=2\cdot T_0$, $T_3=6\cdot T_0$, $T_4=3\cdot T_0$. C) $T_2=3\cdot T_0$, $T_3=5\cdot T_0$, $T_4=2{,}5\cdot T_0$. D) $T_2=2\cdot T_0$, $T_3=5\cdot T_0$, $T_4=2{,}5\cdot T_0$. E) TJY.

Yechim

Grafikdan holatlarning koordinatalarini o’qiymiz: $1:(V_0;,p_0)$, $2:(V_0;,3p_0)$, $3:(2V_0;,3p_0)$, $4:(2V_0;,p_0)$.

Ideal gaz holat tenglamasidan $T=\dfrac{pV}{nR}$, va berilishicha $T_1=\dfrac{p_0V_0}{nR}=T_0$.

2-holat uchun ($V_2=V_0$, $p_2=3p_0$):

$$T_2 = \frac{3p_0\cdot V_0}{nR} = 3\cdot\frac{p_0V_0}{nR} = 3T_0$$

3-holat uchun ($V_3=2V_0$, $p_3=3p_0$):

$$T_3 = \frac{3p_0\cdot 2V_0}{nR} = 6\cdot\frac{p_0V_0}{nR} = 6T_0$$

4-holat uchun ($V_4=2V_0$, $p_4=p_0$):

$$T_4 = \frac{p_0\cdot 2V_0}{nR} = 2\cdot\frac{p_0V_0}{nR} = 2T_0$$

Demak: $T_2=3T_0$, $T_3=6T_0$, $T_4=2T_0$.

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
