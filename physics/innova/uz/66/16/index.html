16-masala

To'yingan bug'. Kritik temperatura

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Quyida to’yingan suv bug’i bosimi va zichligining haroratga bog’lanish jadvali berilgan.

Undan foydalanib jadvalda berkitilgan $x$ va $y$ ma’lumotlarni aniqlang. Suvning molyar massasi 18 g/mol. $R=8,31$ J/(K·mol).

A) $x=0,61$ kPa, $y=20,5$ g/m³. B) $x=0,65$ kPa, $y=19,1$ g/m³. C) $x=0,62$ kPa, $y=18,3$ g/m³. D) $x=0,71$ kPa, $y=19,8$ g/m³. E) $x=0,71$ kPa, $y=18,3$ g/m³.

Yechim

To’yingan bug’ ham (yaxshi yaqinlashishda) ideal gaz qonuniga bo’ysunadi, shuning uchun har bir qatorda bosim $p$ va zichlik $\rho$ orasida quyidagi bog’lanish bor:

$$p = \frac{\rho R T}{M}$$

$x$ ni topamiz ($t=2,^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$, $T=275,15$ K, $\rho_0=5,6$ g/m³ $=0,0056$ kg/m³):

$$x = \frac{\rho_0 R T}{M} = \frac{0,0056\cdot 8,31\cdot 275,15}{0,018}$$

$$x = \frac{12,81}{0,018} \approx 712 \text{ Pa} \approx 0,71 \text{ kPa}$$

$y$ ni topamiz ($t=21,^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$, $T=294,15$ K, $p=2,49$ kPa $=2490$ Pa):

$$y = \rho = \frac{pM}{RT} = \frac{2490\cdot 0,018}{8,31\cdot 294,15}$$

$$y = \frac{44,82}{2444,6} \approx 0,01834 \text{ kg/m}^3 \approx 18,3 \text{ g/m}^3$$

Demak, $x=0,71$ kPa, $y=18,3$ g/m³.

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
