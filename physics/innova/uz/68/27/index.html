27-masala

Kapillarlik hodisalari. Sirt tarangligi

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Sovun pufagining radiusini 1 cm dan 6 cm gacha puflab shishirish uchun necha mJ ish bajarish kerak? Sovun eritmasining sirt taranglik koeffitsienti 45 mN/m ga teng.

A) 4,5. B) 3,96. C) 3,6. D) 1,89. E) 0.

Yechim

Berilgan: $r_1 = 1$ cm $= 0,01$ m, $r_2 = 6$ cm $= 0,06$ m, $\sigma = 45$ mN/m $= 0,045$ N/m.

Sovun pufagining ikkita sirti (ichki va tashqi) bor, shuning uchun umumiy yuza o’zgarishi:

$$\Delta S = 2 \cdot 4\pi (r_2^2 - r_1^2)$$

Bajarilgan ish sirt energiyasi o’zgarishiga teng:

$$A = \sigma \Delta S = 8\pi\sigma (r_2^2 - r_1^2)$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz:

$$A = 8 \cdot 3,14 \cdot 0,045 \cdot (0,06^2 - 0,01^2)$$

$$A = 8 \cdot 3,14 \cdot 0,045 \cdot (0,0036 - 0,0001) = 8 \cdot 3,14 \cdot 0,045 \cdot 0,0035$$

$$A \approx 3,96 \cdot 10^{-3} \text{ J} = 3,96 \text{ mJ}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
