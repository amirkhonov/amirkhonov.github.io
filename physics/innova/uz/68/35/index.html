35-masala

Kapillarlik hodisalari. Sirt tarangligi

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Ichki diametrlari 1 mm va 2 mm bo’lgan ikkita kapillardagi suv sathlarining farqini aniqlang (mm). Suvning sirt taranglik koeffitsienti 72 mN/m.

A) 0. B) 14,4. C) 28,8. D) 43,2. E) 57,6.

Yechim

Berilgan: $d_1 = 1$ mm ($r_1 = 0,5$ mm), $d_2 = 2$ mm ($r_2 = 1$ mm), $\sigma = 72$ mN/m $= 0,072$ N/m, $\rho = 1000$ kg/m³, $g = 10$ m/s².

Kapillar ko’tarilish formulasi:

$$h = \frac{2\sigma}{\rho g r}$$

Birinchi kapillar uchun:

$$h_1 = \frac{2 \cdot 0,072}{1000 \cdot 10 \cdot 0,5 \cdot 10^{-3}} = \frac{0,144}{5} = 0,0288 \text{ m} = 28,8 \text{ mm}$$

Ikkinchi kapillar uchun:

$$h_2 = \frac{2 \cdot 0,072}{1000 \cdot 10 \cdot 10^{-3}} = \frac{0,144}{10} = 0,0144 \text{ m} = 14,4 \text{ mm}$$

Sathlar farqi:

$$\Delta h = h_1 - h_2 = 28,8 - 14,4 = 14,4 \text{ mm}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
