47-masala

Tekis o'zgaruvchan harakatda ko'chish

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Avtomobilning dastlabki tezligi 40 km/h, 100 m yo’lda uning tezligi 60 km/h ga yetgan. Uning tezlanishi (km/h²) qanday?

A) 10. B) 10000. C) 100. D) 1000. E) 1.

Yechim

$v_0=40$ km/h, $v=60$ km/h, $s=100$ m $=0,1$ km (barcha kattaliklarni km va soatda hisoblaymiz, chunki javob km/h² da so’ralgan).

$$v^2 = v_0^2 + 2as ;\Rightarrow; 60^2 = 40^2 + 2a\cdot0,1$$

$$3600 = 1600 + 0,2a ;\Rightarrow; 0,2a = 2000 ;\Rightarrow; a = 10000 \text{ km/h}^2$$

Tekshirish uchun SI birliklarda ham hisoblaymiz: $v_0=11,11$ m/s, $v=16,67$ m/s, $s=100$ m.

$$a = \frac{v^2-v_0^2}{2s} = \frac{277,8-123,5}{200} \approx 0,772 \text{ m/s}^2$$

Bu qiymatni km/h² ga o’tkazsak ($1$ m/s² $= 3600^2/1000 = 12960$ km/h²):

$$a \approx 0,772\cdot 12960 \approx 10000 \text{ km/h}^2$$

Ikkala usul ham bir xil natija — $10000$ km/h² ni beradi, bu esa B) variantiga mos keladi.

Eslatma: kitobdagi javob — A, biroq hisob-kitoblar B (10000 km/h²) javobini tasdiqlaydi. A) variantidagi 10 qiymati berilgan kattaliklarga mos kelmaydi — ehtimol kitobda chop etish xatosi bor.

Javob: B
