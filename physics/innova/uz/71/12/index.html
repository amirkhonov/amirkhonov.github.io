12-masala

Ichki energiya

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Ichki energiyasi 50 kJ bo’lgan neon gazining massasi 1,2 kg ga teng. Uning temperaturasini toping (K). $\mu = 20$ g/mol.

A) 64,45. B) 35,26. C) 66,85. D) 11,15. E) 44,18.

Yechim

Modda miqdori ($m=1200$ g):

$$n = \frac{m}{\mu} = \frac{1200}{20} = 60 \text{ mol}$$

Neon — bir atomli gaz:

$$U = \frac{3}{2}nRT \implies T = \frac{2U}{3nR}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz ($U=50000$ J):

$$T = \frac{2\cdot 50000}{3\cdot 60\cdot 8,31} = \frac{100000}{1495,8} \approx 66,85 \text{ K}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
