Issiqlik miqdori

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Massalari teng bo’lgan 0°C temperaturali muz va 100°C temperaturali suv kalorimetrga solindi. Kalorimetrda qanday harorat (°C) qaror topadi?

A) 6. B) 50. C) 11. D) 24. E) 0.

Yechim

Massalarni $m$ deb belgilaymiz ($m_{muz}=m_{suv}=m$). $c_{suv}=4200$ J/(kg∙K), $\lambda_{muz}=330,000$ J/kg.

Avval tekshiramiz: suv $100°C$ dan $0°C$ gacha sovutilganda ajraladigan maksimal issiqlik $m\cdot4200\cdot100=420,000m$ J, muzni to’liq eritish uchun kerak bo’lgan issiqlik esa $m\cdot330,000=330,000m$ J. Chunki $420,000m > 330,000m$, muz to’liq eriydi va ortiqcha issiqlik aralashmani isitishga sarflanadi.

Issiqlik balansi tenglamasi:

$$mc_{suv}(100-t) = m\lambda_{muz}+mc_{suv}\cdot t$$

$m$ qisqaradi:

$$4200(100-t) = 330,000+4200t$$

$$420,000-4200t = 330,000+4200t$$

$$420,000-330,000 = 4200t+4200t$$

$$90,000 = 8400t$$

$$t = \frac{90,000}{8400} \approx 10,7°C \approx 11°C$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
