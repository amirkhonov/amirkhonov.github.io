129-masala

Issiqlik miqdori

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Stakanda 80°C haroratli 330 g suv bor. Haroratni 50°C gacha tushirish uchun unga harorati 0°C bo’lgan muzdan qancha solish kerak (g)? Stakanning issiqlik sig’imi hisobga olinmasin.

A) 347. B) 205. C) 165. D) 128. E) 77.

Yechim

Berilgan: $m_{suv}=330$ g $=0,33$ kg, $t_{suv}=80°C$; $t_{muz}=0°C$; oxirgi harorat $t=50°C$.

$c_{suv}=4200$ J/(kg∙K), $\lambda_{muz}=330,000$ J/kg.

Suv $80°C$ dan $50°C$ gacha sovutilganda ajratadigan issiqlik:

$$Q = m_{suv}c_{suv}(80-50) = 0,33\cdot4200\cdot30 = 41,580 \text{ J}$$

Bu issiqlik muzni eritish ($m_{muz}\lambda_{muz}$) va hosil bo’lgan suvni $0°C$ dan $50°C$ gacha isitish ($m_{muz}c_{suv}\cdot50$) uchun sarflanadi:

$$Q = m_{muz}\lambda_{muz}+m_{muz}c_{suv}\cdot50 = m_{muz}(330,000+4200\cdot50)$$

$$Q = m_{muz}(330,000+210,000) = m_{muz}\cdot540,000$$

Bundan muz massasini topamiz:

$$m_{muz} = \frac{41,580}{540,000} \approx 0,077 \text{ kg} = 77 \text{ g}$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
