81-masala

Issiqlik miqdori

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

FIK 42% bo’lgan, minutiga 3 g kerosin sarflaydigan isitkichda temperaturasi 31°C bo’lgan 2 l suvni necha minutda qaynatish mumkin? c=4200 J/(kg∙K), q=46 MJ/kg.

A) 6. B) 9. C) 12. D) 10. E) 15.

Yechim

Suvni $31°C$ dan $100°C$ gacha isitish uchun kerak bo’ladigan issiqlik (2 l suv massasi $m=2$ kg):

$$Q = cm\Delta T = 4200 \cdot 2 \cdot (100-31) = 4200 \cdot 2 \cdot 69 = 579600 \text{ J}$$

Har bir minutda yonadigan kerosin $m_1 = 3$ g $= 0,003$ kg ajratadigan to’liq issiqlik:

$$Q_1 = q m_1 = 46\cdot10^6 \cdot 0,003 = 138000 \text{ J}$$

FIK $\eta=0,42$ bo’lgani uchun har minutda suvga uzatiladigan foydali issiqlik:

$$Q_{foyd} = \eta Q_1 = 0,42 \cdot 138000 = 57960 \text{ J/min}$$

Kerakli vaqt:

$$t = \frac{Q}{Q_{foyd}} = \frac{579600}{57960} = 10 \text{ min}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
