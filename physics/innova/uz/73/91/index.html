91-masala

Issiqlik miqdori

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

91*. Massasi 1200 kg bo’lgan avtomobil gorizontal yo’lda 72 km/h tezlikka erishdi. Bunda u 1 km yo’lda 80 g benzin sarflaydi. Benzin uchun q=45∙10⁶ J/kg, dvigatelning FIK 28%. Shu avtomobil yo’lning har 100 m da 3,5 m ko’tariladigan qismida necha km/h tezlikka erishadi? Harakat tekis va qarshilik kuchlari ikki holda ham bir xil deb hisoblang.

A) 62. B) 59. C) 55. D) 51. E) 42.

Yechim

Berilgan: $m = 1200$ kg, $v_1 = 72$ km/h $= 20$ m/s, $1$ km yo’lda $m_{ben}=80$ g $=0,08$ kg benzin, $q=45\cdot10^6$ J/kg, $\eta=0,28$.

Gorizontal yo’lda 1 km masofada ajralgan to’liq issiqlik:

$$Q = q, m_{ben} = 45\cdot10^6 \cdot 0,08 = 3,6\cdot10^6 \text{ J}$$

Shu masofada bajarilgan foydali (mexanik) ish:

$$A_{foyd} = \eta Q = 0,28 \cdot 3,6\cdot10^6 = 1,008\cdot10^6 \text{ J}$$

Tekis harakatda tortishish kuchi qarshilik kuchiga teng bo’lgani uchun, $A_{foyd}=F_{qarsh}\cdot s$ dan qarshilik kuchini topamiz ($s=1000$ m):

$$F_{qarsh} = \frac{A_{foyd}}{s} = \frac{1,008\cdot10^6}{1000} = 1008 \text{ N}$$

Gorizontal yo’ldagi dvigatel quvvati:

$$P = F_{qarsh}, v_1 = 1008 \cdot 20 = 20160 \text{ W}$$

Ko’tarilish burchagi kichik bo’lgani uchun ($100$ m da $3,5$ m ko’tarilish):

$$\sin\theta = \frac{3,5}{100} = 0,035$$

Nishab yo’lda tekis harakat uchun tortish kuchi qarshilik kuchi va og’irlik kuchining yo’l bo’ylab tashkil etuvchisi yig’indisiga teng bo’lishi kerak (qarshilik kuchi ikkala holatda ham bir xil deb berilgan, $g=10$ m/s²):

$$F’ = F_{qarsh} + mg\sin\theta = 1008 + 1200\cdot10\cdot0,035 = 1008 + 420 = 1428 \text{ N}$$

Dvigatel quvvati o’zgarmas ($P=20160$ W) deb, nishabdagi tezlikni topamiz:

$$v_2 = \frac{P}{F’} = \frac{20160}{1428} \approx 14,12 \text{ m/s}$$

Km/h ga o’tkazamiz:

$$v_2 \approx 14,12 \cdot 3,6 \approx 50,8 \text{ km/h} \approx 51 \text{ km/h}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
