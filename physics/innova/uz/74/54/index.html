54-masala

Termodinamikaning birinchi qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Quyidagi ifodalardan qaysi biri izobarik jarayonda bir atomli ideal gazga berilgan issiqlik miqdori hisoblanadi?

  1. $Q = \dfrac{5}{2} \cdot \dfrac{m}{\mu} \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$; 2) $Q = \dfrac{3}{2} \cdot \dfrac{m}{\mu} \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$;
  2. $Q = \dfrac{5}{2} \cdot \nu \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$; 4) $Q = \dfrac{3}{2} \cdot \nu \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$;
  3. $Q = \dfrac{3}{2} \cdot \Delta p \cdot V$; 6) $Q = \dfrac{5}{2} \cdot p \cdot \Delta V$;
  4. $Q = \dfrac{3}{2} \cdot p \cdot \Delta V$; 8) $Q = \dfrac{5}{2} \cdot \Delta p \cdot V$.

A) 1, 2, 3. B) 1, 5, 8. C) 5, 6, 7. D) 5, 7, 8. E) 1, 3, 6.

Yechim

Bir atomli ideal gaz uchun izobarik molyar issiqlik sig’imi:

$$C_p = \frac{5}{2} R$$

Shuning uchun issiqlik miqdori:

$$Q = \frac{5}{2} \cdot \frac{m}{\mu} \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$$

(bu — 1-formula) yoki ekvivalent ravishda:

$$Q = \frac{5}{2} \cdot \nu \cdot R \cdot \Delta T$$

(bu — 3-formula).

Izobarik jarayonda gazning bajargan ishi $A = p\Delta V$ ekanini va $Q = \frac{5}{3}\Delta U$, $A = \nu R \Delta T$ ekanligini hisobga olib, $Q$ ni bosim va hajm o’zgarishi orqali ifodalasak:

$$Q = \frac{5}{2} \cdot p \cdot \Delta V$$

(bu — 6-formula).

Demak, to’g’ri kombinatsiya: 1, 3, 6.

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
