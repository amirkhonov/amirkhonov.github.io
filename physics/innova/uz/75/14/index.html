14-masala

Adiabatik jarayon. Termodinamikaning II qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Adiabatik jarayonda bir atomli ideal gaz ustida tashqi kuchlarning bajargan ishi ifodasi qanday bo’ladi?

A) $A = \dfrac{5}{2}\cdot\dfrac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$. B) $A = \dfrac{5}{3}\cdot\dfrac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$. C) $A = \dfrac{3}{2}\cdot\dfrac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$. D) $A = \dfrac{3}{5}\cdot\dfrac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$. E) $A = \dfrac{2}{3}\cdot\dfrac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$.

Yechim

Adiabatik jarayonda $Q = 0$, shuning uchun tashqi kuchlarning bajargan ishi to’liq gaz ichki energiyasining o’zgarishiga teng bo’ladi:

$$A_{tashqi} = \Delta U$$

Bir atomli ideal gaz uchun ichki energiya ($i=3$ erkinlik darajasi):

$$\Delta U = \frac{3}{2}\cdot\frac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$$

Demak:

$$A_{tashqi} = \frac{3}{2}\cdot\frac{m}{\mu}\cdot R \cdot \Delta T$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
