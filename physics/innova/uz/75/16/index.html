16-masala

Adiabatik jarayon. Termodinamikaning II qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

300 g massali argon (Ar) gazi adiabatik tarzda siqildi. Natijada harorati 400 K dan 480 K gacha ortdi. Bunda gaz qanday ish bajargan (kJ)? $\mu = 0,02$ kg/mol.

A) -45,544. B) -74,654. C) -35,147. D) -14,958. E) 0.

Yechim

Berilgan: $m = 0,300$ kg, $\mu = 0,02$ kg/mol, $T_1 = 400$ K, $T_2 = 480$ K.

Argon — bir atomli gaz ($i = 3$).

Mol miqdori:

$$\nu = \frac{m}{\mu} = \frac{0,300}{0,02} = 15 \text{ mol}$$

Harorat o’zgarishi:

$$\Delta T = 480 - 400 = 80 \text{ K}$$

Ichki energiya o’zgarishi:

$$\Delta U = \frac{3}{2}\nu R \Delta T = \frac{3}{2}\cdot 15 \cdot 8,31 \cdot 80 = 22,5 \cdot 8,31 \cdot 80 = 14958 \text{ J} = 14,958 \text{ kJ}$$

Adiabatik jarayonda $Q=0$, shuning uchun gaz bajargan ish:

$$A = -\Delta U = -14,958 \text{ kJ}$$

(Gaz siqilayotgani uchun uning o’zi manfiy ish bajaradi — ya’ni tashqi kuchlar gaz ustida ish bajaradi.)

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
