63-masala

Issiqlik dvigatellari

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Diagrammada ko’rsatilgan sikl bo’yicha ishlovchi issiqlik mashinasi bir sikl davomida qanday (kJ) ish bajaradi?

A) 3. B) 4. C) 6. D) 7. E) 10.

Yechim

Diagrammadan sikl uchlarining koordinatalari (bosim MPa da, hajm litrda): $(1;1)$, $(1;3)$, $(2;3)$, $(4;1)$.

Bir sikldagi ish sikl chizig’i chegaralagan yuzaga teng. Yuzani Gauss (shnurlash) formulasi bilan hisoblaymiz — nuqtalarni $(V,p)$ tartibida yozamiz: $A(1,1)$, $B(1,3)$, $C(2,3)$, $D(4,1)$:

$$S = \frac{1}{2}\left|\sum (V_i p_{i+1} - V_{i+1} p_i)\right|$$

Har bir juftlik uchun hisoblaymiz:

$$V_A p_B - V_B p_A = 1\cdot3 - 1\cdot1 = 2$$

$$V_B p_C - V_C p_B = 1\cdot3 - 2\cdot3 = -3$$

$$V_C p_D - V_D p_C = 2\cdot1 - 4\cdot3 = -10$$

$$V_D p_A - V_A p_D = 4\cdot1 - 1\cdot1 = 3$$

Yig’indi:

$$2 - 3 - 10 + 3 = -8$$

Yuza (ish):

$$A = \frac{|-8|}{2} = 4 \text{ (MPa}\cdot\text{l)} = 4 \text{ kJ}$$

($1$ MPa$\cdot$l $= 10^6$ Pa $\cdot 10^{-3}$ m$^3 = 1000$ J.)

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
