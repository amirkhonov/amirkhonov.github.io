78-boʻlim · Elektrostatika

27-masala

Kulon qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

27*. Massalari 0,2 g dan bo’lgan 2 ta bir xil sharcha ipga rasmdagidek osilgan. B sharchaga 10 nC zaryad berilgan. C sharchaga necha nC zaryad Berilganda BC ipning taranglik kuchi nolga teng bo’ladi? Bunda AB ipning taranglik kuchi necha mN bo’ladi? BC=3 cm, $k=9\cdot10^9$ N·m²/C².

A) −10 nC, 2 mN. B) 10 nC, 2,5 mN. C) −20 nC, 4 mN. D) 18 nC, 3,5 mN. E) −14 nC, 4 mN.

Yechim

Rasmda A — qattiq nuqta (shift), undan ip bilan B sharcha, B dan ip bilan C sharcha osilgan (vertikal zanjir).

Berilgan: $m = 0,2$ g $= 2\cdot10^{-4}$ kg (har biri), $q_B = 10$ nC, $r_{BC} = 3$ cm $= 0,03$ m, $g=10$ m/s².

C sharchaning zaryadi.

BC ipining taranglik kuchi nolga teng bo’lishi uchun C sharchaga ta’sir etuvchi Kulon kuchi (B tomonidan) uning og’irligini muvozanatlashi kerak, ya’ni C ni yuqoriga tortishi kerak — bu B va C orasida tortishish (demak, qarama-qarshi ishorali zaryadlar) bo’lishini bildiradi.

Muvozanat sharti:

$$k\frac{q_B q_C}{r_{BC}^2} = mg$$

$q_C$ ni topamiz:

$$q_C = \frac{mg, r_{BC}^2}{k, q_B} = \frac{2\cdot10^{-4}\cdot10\cdot0,03^2}{9\cdot10^9\cdot10\cdot10^{-9}}$$

$$q_C = \frac{2\cdot10^{-3}\cdot9\cdot10^{-4}}{90} = \frac{1,8\cdot10^{-6}}{90} = 2\cdot10^{-8}\ \text{C} = 20\ \text{nC}$$

Ishorasi manfiy (B musbat bilan tortishish uchun): $q_C = -20$ nC.

AB ipining taranglik kuchi.

B sharchaga: o’z og’irligi $mg$ (pastga) va C bilan o’zaro ta’sir kuchi ta’sir qiladi. Nyutonning uchinchi qonuniga ko’ra, C ning B ga ko’rsatgan kuchi B ning C ga ko’rsatgan kuchiga teng kattalikda, ya’ni $mg$ ga teng, va B ni pastga (C tomon) tortadi.

AB ipi B va C ning umumiy og’irligini ko’taradi:

$$T_{AB} = mg + mg = 2mg$$

$$T_{AB} = 2\cdot2\cdot10^{-4}\cdot10 = 4\cdot10^{-3}\ \text{N} = 4\ \text{mN}$$

Natija: $q_C = -20$ nC, $T_{AB} = 4$ mN.

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
