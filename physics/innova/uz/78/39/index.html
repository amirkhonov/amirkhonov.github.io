78-boʻlim · Elektrostatika

39-masala

Kulon qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

1-rasmdagi A zaryadga ta’sir qiluvchi natijaviy kuch $F_A$ ga, 2-rasmdagi B zaryadga ta’sir qiluvchi natijaviy kuch esa $F_B$ ga teng. $\dfrac{F_A}{F_B}$ nisbat nimaga teng?

A) 1. B) 2. C) 1,5. D) 3. E) 4.

Yechim

1-rasm. Uchburchakning uchida $+2q$ (A), asosida ikkita $+q$ zaryad, A dagi tomonlar $a$ ga teng, asos burchaklari $30°+30°$, demak A dagi burchak $120°$.

Har bir asosdagi $+q$ dan A ga ta’sir etuvchi kuch:

$$f = k\frac{2q\cdot q}{a^2} = \frac{2kq^2}{a^2}$$

Ikkala kuch orasidagi burchak A dagi uchburchak burchagiga teng — $120°$. Ikki teng vektorning yig’indisi:

$$F_A = 2f\cos\frac{120°}{2} = 2f\cos60° = 2f\cdot0,5 = f = \frac{2kq^2}{a^2}$$

2-rasm. Teng tomonli uchburchak (barcha burchaklar $60°$), uchida $+q$ (B), asosida $+q$ va $-q$, tomonlar $a$ ga teng.

Asosdagi $+q$ dan B ga itarishish kuchi, asosdagi $-q$ dan B ga tortishish kuchi — ikkalasi ham kattaligi bo’yicha teng:

$$f’ = k\frac{q\cdot q}{a^2} = \frac{kq^2}{a^2}$$

Geometrik tahlil shuni ko’rsatadiki, itarishish va tortishish kuchlari orasidagi burchak $180°-60°=120°$ ga teng (chunki itarishish kuchi manba zaryaddan uzoqlashish, tortishish kuchi esa manba zaryad tomon yo’nalgan).

$$F_B = 2f’\cos60° = f’ = \frac{kq^2}{a^2}$$

Nisbat.

$$\frac{F_A}{F_B} = \frac{2kq^2/a^2}{kq^2/a^2} = 2$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
