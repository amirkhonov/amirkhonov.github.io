Zaryadlari $q_1=q_2=10^{-8}$ C boβÄôlgan ikki sharcha shiftga yopishtirib qoβÄôyilgan. Massasi 8,7 mg boβÄôlgan uchinchi sharcha havoda muallaq turishi uchun uning zaryadi qanday ¬© boβÄôlishi kerak? Sharchalar tomonlari 3 cm dan boβÄôlgan muntazam uchburchakning uchlarida joylashgan. $\sqrt3 = 1,74$, $g=9,8$ m/―¹¬≤.

A) $+9,8\cdot10^{-10}$. B) $-4,9\cdot10^{-10}$. C) $+4,9\cdot10^{-10}$. D) $-9,8\cdot10^{-10}$. E) muvozanat zaryad qiymatiga bogβÄôliq emas.

Yechim

Berilgan: $q_1=q_2=10^{-8}$ C (shiftda, teng tomonli uchburchakning yuqori uchlarida), $m_3 = 8,7$ mg $= 8,7\cdot10^{-6}$ kg (havoda muallaq turadigan sharcha), $a = 3$ cm $= 0,03$ m (uchburchak tomoni), $\sqrt3=1,74$, $g=9,8$ m/―¹¬≤.

$q_3$ havoda ip yoki tayanchsiz muallaq turishi uchun, $q_1$ va $q_2$ dan unga taβÄôsir etuvchi Kulon kuchlarining tik (vertikal) tashkil etuvchilari yigβÄôindisi $q_3$ ning ogβÄôirligini muvozanatlashi kerak.

Bu faqat tortishish kuchi (yaβÄôni $q_3$ ishorasi $q_1, q_2$ nikiga qarama-qarshi, manfiy) yuqoriga yoβÄônalganda mumkin, chunki itarishish $q_3$ ni pastga (shiftdan uzoqroqqa) itaradi.

Muntazam (teng tomonli) uchburchakda $q_1$-$q_3$ va $q_2$-$q_3$ tomonlari asos bilan $60¬Α$ burchak hosil qiladi, shuning uchun har bir kuchning vertikal tashkil etuvchisi $F_0\sin60¬Α = F_0\cdot\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}$ ga teng.

Ikkala kuchning vertikal tashkil etuvchilari yigβÄôindisi ogβÄôirlikka teng:

$$2F_0\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{2} = m_3 g \implies F_0\sqrt3 = m_3 g \implies F_0 = \frac{m_3 g}{\sqrt3}$$

Qiymatlarni qoβÄôyamiz:

$$m_3 g = 8,7\cdot10^{-6}\cdot9,8 = 8,526\cdot10^{-5}\ \text{N}$$

$$F_0 = \frac{8,526\cdot10^{-5}}{1,74} = 4,9\cdot10^{-5}\ \text{N}$$

Kulon qonunidan $q_3$ ni topamiz:

$$F_0 = k\frac{q_1, |q_3|}{a^2} \implies |q_3| = \frac{F_0 a^2}{k, q_1}$$

$$|q_3| = \frac{4,9\cdot10^{-5}\cdot0,03^2}{9\cdot10^9\cdot10^{-8}} = \frac{4,9\cdot10^{-5}\cdot9\cdot10^{-4}}{90}$$

$$|q_3| = \frac{4,41\cdot10^{-8}}{90} = 4,9\cdot10^{-10}\ \text{C}$$

Ishorasi manfiy: $q_3 = -4,9\cdot10^{-10}$ C.

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
