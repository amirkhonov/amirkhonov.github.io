12-masala

Elektr maydondagi dielektriklar

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Zaryadlari q va 0,5 μC bo’lgan zaryadlangan nuqtaviy zarralar slyudada bir-biriga 250 N Kulon kuchlari bilan ta’sirlashadi. Zarralar orasidagi masofa 3 cm bo’lsa, q ning qiymatini toping (μC). Slyudaning dielektrik singdiruvchanligi 6 ga teng.

A) 150. B) 300. C) 420. D) 225. E) 540.

Yechim

Berilgan: $F = 250$ N, $q_2 = 0{,}5\cdot10^{-6}$ Kl, $r = 0{,}03$ m, $\varepsilon = 6$.

Kulon qonuni:

$$F = \frac{kqq_2}{\varepsilon r^2}$$

Bundan $q$ ni ifodalaymiz:

$$q = \frac{F\varepsilon r^2}{kq_2} = \frac{250 \cdot 6 \cdot 0{,}03^2}{9\cdot10^9 \cdot 0{,}5\cdot10^{-6}}$$

Hisoblaymiz:

$$q = \frac{250\cdot6\cdot0{,}0009}{4500} = \frac{1{,}35}{4500} = 3\cdot10^{-4} \text{ Kl} = 300 \text{ μC}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
