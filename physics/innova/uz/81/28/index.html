28-masala

Elektr maydondagi dielektriklar

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Ikki sharcha bir xil uzunlikdagi iplar yordamida bir nuqtaga osilgan va bir xil ishora bilan zaryadlangan. Sharchalar zichligi ρ va dielektrik singdiruvchanligi ε bo’lgan suyuqlikka botirilganda, iplar orasidagi burchak o’zgarmasligi uchun sharchalarning zichligi qanday bo’lishi kerak?

A) $\rho\left(1+\dfrac{1}{\varepsilon}\right)$. B) $\dfrac{\rho\varepsilon}{\varepsilon-1}$. C) $\dfrac{\rho(\varepsilon-1)}{\varepsilon}$. D) $\rho(1+\varepsilon)$. E) $\rho\varepsilon(\varepsilon-1)$.

Yechim

Havoda sharchalar orasidagi Kulon kuchi $F$ va og’irlik kuchi $mg$ nisbati burchakni belgilaydi:

$$\tan\frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{F}{mg}$$

Suyuqlikka botirilganda Kulon kuchi $\varepsilon$ marta kamayadi ($F’ = F/\varepsilon$), og’irlik kuchiga esa Arximed kuchi qarshi ta’sir qiladi:

$$\tan\frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{F/\varepsilon}{mg - F_{Arx}}$$

Burchak o’zgarmasligi uchun ikkala ifoda teng bo’lishi kerak:

$$\frac{F}{mg} = \frac{F/\varepsilon}{mg-F_{Arx}}$$

Bundan:

$$mg - F_{Arx} = \frac{mg}{\varepsilon} \quad\Rightarrow\quad F_{Arx} = mg\left(1-\frac{1}{\varepsilon}\right) = mg\cdot\frac{\varepsilon-1}{\varepsilon}$$

Sharcha massasi $m=\rho_{shar}V$, Arximed kuchi $F_{Arx}=\rho V g$ (bu yerda $\rho$ — suyuqlik zichligi):

$$\rho V g = \rho_{shar}Vg\cdot\frac{\varepsilon-1}{\varepsilon}$$

$$\rho = \rho_{shar}\cdot\frac{\varepsilon-1}{\varepsilon} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \rho_{shar} = \frac{\rho\varepsilon}{\varepsilon-1}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
