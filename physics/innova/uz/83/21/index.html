83-boʻlim · Elektrostatika

21-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

$q_1=5\cdot10^{-9}$ va $q_2=-6\cdot10^{-9}$ C nuqtaviy zaryadlar orasidagi masofa 6 cm ga teng. Zaryadlar o’rtasida joylashgan nuqtadagi $E$ kuchlanganlikni va $\varphi$ potensialni toping. Muhit – havo.

A) $11\cdot10^4$ V/m, φ=−300 V. B) $11\cdot10^4$ V/m, φ=300 V. C) $14\cdot10^3$ V/m, φ=−100 V. D) $14\cdot10^3$ V/m, φ=100 V. E) $5\cdot10^4$ V/m, φ=−200 V.

Yechim

Nuqta ikkala zaryaddan $r=3$ cm$=0,03$ m masofada.

$q_1$ dan hosil bo’lgan kuchlanganlik (undan uzoqlashish yo’nalishida):

$$E_1 = \frac{kq_1}{r^2} = \frac{9\cdot10^9 \cdot 5\cdot10^{-9}}{0,03^2} = \frac{45}{0,0009} = 5\cdot10^4 \text{ V/m}$$

$q_2$ dan hosil bo’lgan kuchlanganlik ($q_2$ manfiy bo’lgani uchun uning tomon yo’nalgan, ya’ni $E_1$ bilan bir xil yo’nalishda):

$$E_2 = \frac{k|q_2|}{r^2} = \frac{9\cdot10^9 \cdot 6\cdot10^{-9}}{0,0009} = \frac{54}{0,0009} = 6\cdot10^4 \text{ V/m}$$

Ikkala vektor bir xil yo’nalishda bo’lgani uchun natijaviy kuchlanganlik:

$$E = E_1 + E_2 = 5\cdot10^4 + 6\cdot10^4 = 11\cdot10^4 \text{ V/m}$$

Potensial (skalyar yig’indi):

$$\varphi = \frac{k(q_1+q_2)}{r} = \frac{9\cdot10^9 \cdot (5\cdot10^{-9} - 6\cdot10^{-9})}{0,03} = \frac{-9}{0,03} = -300 \text{ V}$$

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
