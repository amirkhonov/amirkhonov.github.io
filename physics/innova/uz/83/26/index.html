26-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

$+2q$ va $-q$ zaryadlar rasmdagi kabi o’rnatilgan. M va N nuqtalar orasidagi potensiallar farqi nimaga teng?

A) $2kq/a$. B) $2kq/3a$. C) $3kq/2a$. D) $7kq/6a$. E) 0.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra: $+2q$ dan M gacha masofa $a$, $+2q$ dan N gacha masofa $2a$, $-q$ dan N gacha masofa $2a$, $-q$ dan M gacha masofa $3a$.

M nuqtadagi potensial:

$$\varphi_M = \frac{k\cdot2q}{a} + \frac{k(-q)}{3a} = kq\left(\frac{2}{a}-\frac{1}{3a}\right) = kq\cdot\frac{5}{3a} = \frac{5kq}{3a}$$

N nuqtadagi potensial:

$$\varphi_N = \frac{k\cdot2q}{2a} + \frac{k(-q)}{2a} = kq\left(\frac{1}{a}-\frac{1}{2a}\right) = \frac{kq}{2a}$$

Potensiallar farqi:

$$U_{MN} = \varphi_M - \varphi_N = \frac{5kq}{3a} - \frac{kq}{2a} = \frac{10kq}{6a} - \frac{3kq}{6a} = \frac{7kq}{6a}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
