83-boʻlim · Elektrostatika

27-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

$+3q$ va $+4q$ zaryadlar rasmdagi kabi o’rnatilgan. M va N nuqtalar orasidagi potensiallar farqi nimaga teng?

A) $kq/15a$. B) $2kq/15a$. C) $kq/5a$. D) $4kq/15a$. E) $kq/3a$.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra to’g’ri to’rtburchak hosil bo’ladi: $+3q$ dan M gacha (tik tomon) $3a$, $+3q$ dan $+4q$ gacha (asos) $4a$, shuning uchun diagonallar:

$$d = \sqrt{(3a)^2+(4a)^2} = 5a$$

M nuqtadagi potensial ($+3q$ gacha $3a$, $+4q$ gacha diagonal $5a$):

$$\varphi_M = \frac{k\cdot3q}{3a} + \frac{k\cdot4q}{5a} = \frac{kq}{a} + \frac{4kq}{5a} = \frac{5kq}{5a}+\frac{4kq}{5a} = \frac{9kq}{5a}$$

N nuqtadagi potensial ($+3q$ gacha diagonal $5a$, $+4q$ gacha tik tomon $3a$):

$$\varphi_N = \frac{k\cdot3q}{5a} + \frac{k\cdot4q}{3a} = \frac{9kq}{15a}+\frac{20kq}{15a} = \frac{29kq}{15a}$$

Potensiallar farqi (moduli):

$$|U_{MN}| = \left|\frac{9kq}{5a} - \frac{29kq}{15a}\right| = \left|\frac{27kq}{15a} - \frac{29kq}{15a}\right| = \frac{2kq}{15a}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
