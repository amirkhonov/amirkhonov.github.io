30-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

O nuqtadagi natijaviy elektr maydon kuchlanganligi 60 N/C ga teng bo’lsa, shu nuqtadagi natijaviy potensialni aniqlang (V)? $x = 3$ m.

A) 180. B) 60. C) 120. D) 40. E) 50.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra $+3q$ dan O gacha masofa $x$ (gorizontal), $+4q$ dan O gacha masofa $x$ (vertikal), $-8q$ dan O gacha masofa $2x$ bo’lib, $-8q$ ga tortilgan chiziq gorizontal yo’nalish bilan $37°$ burchak hosil qiladi.

$+3q$ dan kuchlanganlik (O da undan uzoqlashish yo’nalishida, gorizontal o’qqa yo’nalgan):

$$E_3 = \frac{k\cdot3q}{x^2}, \quad \text{yo’nalishi } (1,0)$$

$+4q$ dan kuchlanganlik (vertikal pastga):

$$E_4 = \frac{k\cdot4q}{x^2}, \quad \text{yo’nalishi } (0,-1)$$

$-8q$ dan kuchlanganlik (manfiy zaryad tomon, ya’ni $37°$ burchak bo’yicha, vertikaldan hisoblanganda):

$$E_8 = \frac{k\cdot8q}{(2x)^2} = \frac{2kq}{x^2}, \quad \text{yo’nalishi } (-\sin37°,\cos37°)=(-0,6;\ 0,8)$$

Tashkil etuvchilar yig’indisi ($kq/x^2 = a$ desak):

$$E_x = 3a - 0,6\cdot2a = 1,8a$$

$$E_y = -4a + 0,8\cdot2a = -2,4a$$

Natijaviy kuchlanganlik:

$$E = a\sqrt{1,8^2+2,4^2} = a\sqrt{3,24+5,76} = a\sqrt{9} = 3a = \frac{3kq}{x^2}$$

Berilgan $E=60$ N/C, $x=3$ m dan:

$$\frac{3kq}{x^2}=60 \Rightarrow kq = 20x^2 = 20\cdot9 = 180$$

Natijaviy potensial:

$$\varphi = \frac{k\cdot3q}{x} + \frac{k\cdot4q}{x} - \frac{k\cdot8q}{2x} = \frac{7kq}{x} - \frac{4kq}{x} = \frac{3kq}{x} = \frac{3\cdot180}{3} = 180 \text{ V}$$

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
