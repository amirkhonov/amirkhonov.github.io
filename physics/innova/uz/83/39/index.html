39-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

9 cm radiusli sharning sirtida 0,1 nC musbat zaryad tekis taqsimlangan. Sharning markazidagi kuchlanganlik va potensialni aniqlang.

A) E=200 V/m; φ=10 V. B) $E=1,1\cdot10^2$ V/m; φ=0. C) E=1,1 V/m; φ=10 V. D) E=0; φ=10 V. E) $E=1,1\cdot10^3$ V/m; φ=10 V.

Yechim

Berilgan: $R=9$ cm$=0,09$ m, $Q=0,1$ nC$=1\cdot10^{-10}$ Kl.

Zaryad faqat sirtda taqsimlangani uchun ichkarida (markazda) elektr maydon kuchlanganligi nolga teng:

$$E = 0$$

Potensial esa ichkarida o’zgarmas va sirtdagi qiymatga teng:

$$\varphi = \frac{kQ}{R} = \frac{9\cdot10^9\cdot1\cdot10^{-10}}{0,09} = \frac{0,9}{0,09} = 10 \text{ V}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
