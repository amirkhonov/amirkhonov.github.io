45-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

$\varphi_0$ potensialgacha zaryadlangan $n$ ta bir xil sharsimon tomchilarning qo’shilishi natijasida hosil bo’lgan katta tomchining potensiali qanday bo’ladi?

A) $n^2\varphi_0$. B) $n\varphi_0$. C) $\sqrt[3]{n}\varphi_0$. D) $\sqrt{n^3}\varphi_0$. E) $\sqrt[3]{n^2}\varphi_0$.

Yechim

Kichik tomchi radiusi $r$, zaryadi $q$, potensiali:

$$\varphi_0 = \frac{kq}{r}$$

$n$ ta tomchi qo’shilganda hajm saqlanadi:

$$n\cdot\frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 = \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3 \Rightarrow R = n^{1/3}r$$

Zaryad esa yig’indi bo’ladi: $Q=nq$.

Katta tomchining potensiali:

$$\varphi = \frac{kQ}{R} = \frac{k\cdot nq}{n^{1/3}r} = n^{2/3}\cdot\frac{kq}{r} = n^{2/3}\varphi_0 = \sqrt[3]{n^2},\varphi_0$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
