60-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Rasmdagi x sharning elektr maydon kuchlanganligi $E$ ga, y sharniki $2E$ ga teng.

Sharlar bir-biriga tekkizib olingandan keyin x sharning elektr maydon kuchlanganligi nimaga teng bo’lib qoladi?

A) E/3. B) 2E/3. C) E. D) 2E. E) 3E.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra x sharning radiusi $R$, y sharning radiusi $2R$.

Sirtdagi kuchlanganlik orqali zaryadlarni topamiz:

$$E = \frac{kq_x}{R^2} \Rightarrow q_x = \frac{ER^2}{k}$$

$$2E = \frac{kq_y}{(2R)^2} \Rightarrow q_y = \frac{2E\cdot4R^2}{k} = \frac{8ER^2}{k}$$

Umumiy zaryad:

$$Q_{\Sigma} = \frac{ER^2}{k} + \frac{8ER^2}{k} = \frac{9ER^2}{k}$$

Tekkizilgandan so’ng umumiy potensial:

$$\varphi’ = \frac{kQ_{\Sigma}}{R+2R} = \frac{9ER^2}{3R} = 3ER$$

x shardagi yangi zaryad:

$$q_x’ = \frac{\varphi’ R}{k} = \frac{3ER\cdot R}{k} = \frac{3ER^2}{k}$$

x sharning yangi sirt kuchlanganligi:

$$E_x’ = \frac{kq_x’}{R^2} = \frac{k\cdot\dfrac{3ER^2}{k}}{R^2} = 3E$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
