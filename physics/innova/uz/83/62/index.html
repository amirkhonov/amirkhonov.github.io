62-masala

Nuqtaviy zaryad va zaryadlangan sharning potensiali

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Rasmdagi K shar avval L sharga, keyin M sharga tekkizib olindi.Shundan so’ng K sharning zaryadi nimaga teng bo’lib qoladi?

A) +q. B) +3q. C) +2q. D) +6q. E) +5q.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra: K shar $+4q$, radiusi $R$; L shar $+2q$, radiusi $R$; M shar $+12q$, radiusi $2R$.

1-qadam: K sharni L sharga tekkizamiz (radiuslar teng bo’lgani uchun zaryad teng taqsimlanadi):

$$Q = 4q+2q = 6q \Rightarrow K’ = L’ = 3q$$

2-qadam: K sharni ($3q$, radiusi $R$) M sharga ($12q$, radiusi $2R$) tekkizamiz.

$$Q = 3q+12q = 15q$$

Nisbat $R:2R=1:3$ (yig’indi 3 ulush):

$$K’’ = 15q\cdot\frac{1}{3} = 5q$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
