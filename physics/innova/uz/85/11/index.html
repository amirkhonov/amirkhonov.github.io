11-masala

E va φ orasidagi bog'lanish

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

$10^{-9}$ g massali chang zarrachasi gorizontal joylashgan plastinalar orasida muallaq turibdi. Plastinalardagi kuchlanish 1 kV, ular orasidagi masofa 5 cm bo’lsa, zarrachaning zaryadi necha Kulonga teng?

A) $10^{-10}$. B) $10^{-12}$. C) $10^{-16}$. D) $10^{-17}$. E) $5\cdot10^{-16}$.

Yechim

Berilgan: massa $m = 10^{-9}$ g $= 10^{-12}$ kg, kuchlanish $U = 1$ kV $= 1000$ V, masofa $d = 5$ cm $= 0{,}05$ m.

Zarracha muallaq turishi uchun elektr kuchi og’irlik kuchini muvozanatlashi kerak:

$$qE = mg$$

Maydon kuchlanganligi:

$$E = \frac{U}{d} = \frac{1000}{0{,}05} = 2\cdot10^4 \text{ V/m}$$

Zaryadni topamiz:

$$q = \frac{mg}{E} = \frac{10^{-12}\cdot10}{2\cdot10^4} = 5\cdot10^{-16} \text{ C}$$

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
