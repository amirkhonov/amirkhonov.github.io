11-masala

Zaryadlangan kondensator energiyasi

Toʻgʻri javob A

Savol

Kondensator energiyasini 2 marta oshirish uchun kuchlanishni qanday o’zgartirish kerak?

A) $\sqrt{2}$ marta oshirish. B) 4 marta oshirish. C) 2 marta kamaytirish. D) 2 marta oshirish. E) 1,4 marta kamaytirish.

Yechim

Sig’im o’zgarmas bo’lgani uchun:

$$W = \frac{CU^2}{2} \implies W \sim U^2$$

Energiyani 2 marta oshirish uchun:

$$\frac{W_2}{W_1} = \left(\frac{U_2}{U_1}\right)^2 = 2 \implies \frac{U_2}{U_1} = \sqrt{2}$$

Demak, kuchlanishni $\sqrt{2}$ marta oshirish kerak.

Bu A javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: A
