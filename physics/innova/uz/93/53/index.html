53-masala

O'zgarmas tokning ishi va quvvati

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Rasmda kuchlanish manbaiga ulangan 4 ta lampochka tasvirlangan. Qaysi lampochkada eng katta quvvat ajraladi? Agar U=100 V bo’lsa, shu quvvatni toping.

A) 1-lampochkada 160 W. B) 3-lampochkada 2500 W. C) 4-lampochkada 192 W. D) 2- va 4-lampochkada bir xil 250 W. E) 1- va 3-lampochkada bir xil 180 W.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra 1-lampochka ($R_1=10$ Ω) ketma-ket, unga 2- va 3-lampochkalar ($R_2=12$ Ω, $R_3=4$ Ω) parallel, so’ngra 4-lampochka ($R_4=12$ Ω) yana ketma-ket ulangan.

2- va 3-lampochkalarning parallel qarshiligi:

$$R_{23} = \frac{R_2 R_3}{R_2+R_3} = \frac{12 \cdot 4}{12+4} = 3 \text{ Ω}$$

Zanjirning umumiy qarshiligi:

$$R = R_1 + R_{23} + R_4 = 10 + 3 + 12 = 25 \text{ Ω}$$

Umumiy (1- va 4-lampochkalardan o’tadigan) tok kuchi:

$$I = \frac{U}{R} = \frac{100}{25} = 4 \text{ A}$$

1-lampochkadagi quvvat:

$$P_1 = I^2 R_1 = 4^2 \cdot 10 = 160 \text{ W}$$

4-lampochkadagi quvvat:

$$P_4 = I^2 R_4 = 4^2 \cdot 12 = 192 \text{ W}$$

Parallel qism ($R_{23}$) dagi kuchlanish:

$$U_{23} = IR_{23} = 4 \cdot 3 = 12 \text{ V}$$

2-lampochkadagi tok va quvvat:

$$I_2 = \frac{U_{23}}{R_2} = \frac{12}{12} = 1 \text{ A} \Rightarrow P_2 = I_2^2 R_2 = 1^2 \cdot 12 = 12 \text{ W}$$

3-lampochkadagi tok va quvvat:

$$I_3 = \frac{U_{23}}{R_3} = \frac{12}{4} = 3 \text{ A} \Rightarrow P_3 = I_3^2 R_3 = 3^2 \cdot 4 = 36 \text{ W}$$

Taqqoslasak, eng katta quvvat 4-lampochkada: $P_4 = 192$ W.

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
