54-masala

O'zgarmas tokning ishi va quvvati

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Chizmada berilgan zanjirdagi elektr tokining quvvati qanday (W). U=12 V, R₁=7 Ω, R₂=5 Ω, R₃=R₅=4 Ω, R₄=9 Ω.

A) 40. B) 36. C) 48. D) 42. E) 24.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra $R_1$ va $R_2$ ketma-ket ulanib, ularga $R_3$ parallel ulangan; bu birlashmaga $R_4$ ketma-ket ulangan; hosil bo’lgan butun tarmoqqa esa $R_5$ parallel ulangan.

$R_1$ va $R_2$ ning ketma-ket yig’indisi:

$$R_{12} = R_1 + R_2 = 7 + 5 = 12 \text{ Ω}$$

$R_{12}$ va $R_3$ ning parallel qarshiligi:

$$R_{123} = \frac{R_{12} R_3}{R_{12}+R_3} = \frac{12 \cdot 4}{12+4} = 3 \text{ Ω}$$

$R_4$ bilan ketma-ket ulanganda:

$$R_{1234} = R_{123} + R_4 = 3 + 9 = 12 \text{ Ω}$$

$R_5$ bilan parallel ulanganda umumiy qarshilik:

$$R = \frac{R_{1234} R_5}{R_{1234}+R_5} = \frac{12 \cdot 4}{12+4} = 3 \text{ Ω}$$

Zanjirdagi tok quvvati:

$$P = \frac{U^2}{R} = \frac{12^2}{3} = 48 \text{ W}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
