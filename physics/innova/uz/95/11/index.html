11-masala

Lampalarni ketma-ket va parallel ulash

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

$P$ va $2P$ quvvatga ega bo’lgan, bir xil kuchlanishga mo’ljallangan ikkita lampa tarmoqqa ketma-ket ulansa, birinchi lampa qanday quvvat bilan yonadi?

A) $P/9$. B) $4P/9$. C) $2P/9$. D) $P/2$. E) $2P/3$.

Yechim

Lampalar $U_0$ kuchlanishga mo’ljallangan, tarmoq kuchlanishi ham $U_0$.

Qarshiliklari:

$$R_1=\frac{U_0^2}{P}, \qquad R_2=\frac{U_0^2}{2P}=\frac{R_1}{2}$$

Umumiy qarshilik:

$$R=R_1+R_2=1,5R_1$$

Tok kuchi:

$$I=\frac{U_0}{1,5R_1}$$

Birinchi lampada ajraladigan quvvat:

$$P_1=I^2R_1=\frac{U_0^2}{(1,5R_1)^2}\cdot R_1=\frac{U_0^2}{2,25R_1}=\frac{P}{2,25}=\frac{4P}{9}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
