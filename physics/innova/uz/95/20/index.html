20-masala

Lampalarni ketma-ket va parallel ulash

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

$P_1=50$ W, $P_2=25$ W, $P_3=100$ W va $P_4=50$ W quvvatga va bir xil kuchlanishga mo’ljallangan $L_1$, $L_2$, $L_3$ va $L_4$ elektr chiroqlar tarmoqqa rasmda ko’rsatilgandek ulangan. Ularning qaysi birida eng ko’p issiqlik ajraladi?

A) hamma chiroqlarda bir xil ajraladi. B) $L_2$ da. C) $L_3$ da. D) $L_4$ da. E) $L_1$ da.

Yechim

Rasmga ko’ra $L_1$ va $L_2$ o’zaro parallel bo’lib bitta blokni, $L_3$ va $L_4$ o’zaro parallel bo’lib ikkinchi blokni tashkil qiladi, ikkala blok esa o’zaro ketma-ket ulangan.

Har bir chiroqning qarshiligi $R=\dfrac{U_0^2}{P}$ dan:

$$R_1=\frac{U_0^2}{50}, \quad R_2=\frac{U_0^2}{25}, \quad R_3=\frac{U_0^2}{100}, \quad R_4=\frac{U_0^2}{50}$$

1-blok ($L_1 \parallel L_2$) qarshiligi:

$$\frac{1}{R_A}=\frac{1}{R_1}+\frac{1}{R_2}=\frac{50}{U_0^2}+\frac{25}{U_0^2}=\frac{75}{U_0^2} \implies R_A=\frac{U_0^2}{75}$$

2-blok ($L_3 \parallel L_4$) qarshiligi:

$$\frac{1}{R_B}=\frac{1}{R_3}+\frac{1}{R_4}=\frac{100}{U_0^2}+\frac{50}{U_0^2}=\frac{150}{U_0^2} \implies R_B=\frac{U_0^2}{150}$$

Bloklar ketma-ket ulangani uchun ulardan bir xil tok $I$ o’tadi, shuning uchun bloklardagi kuchlanishlar $R_A=2R_B$ ga proporsional: $U_A=2U_B$.

Blok ichida chiroqlar parallel, ya’ni kuchlanish bir xil. $L_1$ va $L_2$ uchun ($U_A$ kuchlanishda):

$$P_{L1}=\frac{U_A^2}{R_1}=\frac{50U_A^2}{U_0^2}, \qquad P_{L2}=\frac{U_A^2}{R_2}=\frac{25U_A^2}{U_0^2}$$

$L_3$ va $L_4$ uchun ($U_B$ kuchlanishda):

$$P_{L3}=\frac{U_B^2}{R_3}=\frac{100U_B^2}{U_0^2}, \qquad P_{L4}=\frac{U_B^2}{R_4}=\frac{50U_B^2}{U_0^2}$$

$U_A=2U_B$ bo’lgani uchun $U_A^2=4U_B^2$. Solishtirish uchun barchasini $U_B^2$ orqali ifodalaymiz:

$$P_{L1}=\frac{200U_B^2}{U_0^2}, \quad P_{L2}=\frac{100U_B^2}{U_0^2}, \quad P_{L3}=\frac{100U_B^2}{U_0^2}, \quad P_{L4}=\frac{50U_B^2}{U_0^2}$$

Eng katta qiymat $P_{L1}$ da.

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
