100-masala

To'liq zanjir uchun Om qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob D

Savol

Ushbu elektr zanjirdagi 6 Ω qarshilikdan ajralayotgan quvvatni aniqlan (W).

A) 72. B) 82. C) 90. D) 96. E) 108.

Yechim

Berilgan: $R_1 = 6$ Ω, $R_2 = 3$ Ω, $R_3 = 2$ Ω, $\varepsilon = 60$ V, $r = 1$ Ω.

Rasmga ko’ra $R_1$ va $R_2$ o’zaro parallel ulangan, bu birikma esa $R_3$ ga ketma-ket ulangan.

$R_1$ va $R_2$ ning parallel birikmasi:

$$R_{12} = \frac{R_1 R_2}{R_1+R_2} = \frac{6 \cdot 3}{6+3} = 2 \text{ Ω}$$

Zanjirning to’la qarshiligi:

$$R_{umumiy} = R_{12} + R_3 + r = 2+2+1 = 5 \text{ Ω}$$

Zanjirdagi umumiy tok kuchi:

$$I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R_{umumiy}} = \frac{60}{5} = 12 \text{ A}$$

$R_{12}$ birikmadagi kuchlanish:

$$U_{12} = I \cdot R_{12} = 12 \cdot 2 = 24 \text{ V}$$

$R_1$ dan o’tayotgan tok kuchi:

$$I_1 = \frac{U_{12}}{R_1} = \frac{24}{6} = 4 \text{ A}$$

$R_1$ dagi quvvat:

$$P_1 = I_1^2 R_1 = 4^2 \cdot 6 = 96 \text{ W}$$

Bu D javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: D
