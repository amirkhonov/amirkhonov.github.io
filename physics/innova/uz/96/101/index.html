101-masala

To'liq zanjir uchun Om qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Ushbu elektr zanjirdagi $R_1$, $R_4$ qarshiliklardan, tok manbaidan hamda butun elektr zanjirdan 3 s da ajralib chiqayotgan issiqlikni aniqlang (J).

A) 30; 26; 106; 450. B) 12; 30; 16; 180. C) 32; 36; 96; 480. D) 14; 25; 38; 100. E) 35; 68; 102; 650.

Yechim

Berilgan: $R_1 = 6$ Ω, $R_2 = 3$ Ω, $R_3 = 12$ Ω, $R_4 = 12$ Ω, $R_5 = 8$ Ω, $\varepsilon = 40$ V, $r = 2$ Ω, $t = 3$ s.

Rasmga ko’ra $R_1$ va $R_2$ o’zaro parallel ulangan (1-guruh). Undan keyin $R_3$ va $R_4$ ketma-ket ulanib, bu birikma $R_5$ ga parallel ulangan (2-guruh), va bu ikki guruh o’zaro ketma-ket.

1-guruh:

$$R_{12} = \frac{R_1 R_2}{R_1+R_2} = \frac{6 \cdot 3}{9} = 2 \text{ Ω}$$

2-guruh — $R_3$ va $R_4$ ketma-ket birikmasi $R_5$ ga parallel:

$$R_{345} = \frac{(R_3+R_4) \cdot R_5}{(R_3+R_4)+R_5} = \frac{24 \cdot 8}{24+8} = \frac{192}{32} = 6 \text{ Ω}$$

Zanjirning to’la qarshiligi:

$$R_{umumiy} = R_{12} + R_{345} + r = 2+6+2 = 10 \text{ Ω}$$

Umumiy tok kuchi:

$$I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R_{umumiy}} = \frac{40}{10} = 4 \text{ A}$$

$R_{12}$ dagi kuchlanish $U_{12} = I \cdot R_{12} = 4 \cdot 2 = 8$ V, shundan:

$$I_1 = \frac{U_{12}}{R_1} = \frac{8}{6} = \frac{4}{3} \text{ A}$$

$R_{345}$ dagi kuchlanish $U_{345} = I \cdot R_{345} = 4 \cdot 6 = 24$ V. $R_3$ va $R_4$ ketma-ket bo’lgani uchun ular orqali bir xil tok o’tadi:

$$I_4 = \frac{U_{345}}{R_3+R_4} = \frac{24}{24} = 1 \text{ A}$$

$R_1$ dagi issiqlik:

$$Q_1 = I_1^2 R_1 t = \left(\frac{4}{3}\right)^2 \cdot 6 \cdot 3 = 32 \text{ J}$$

$R_4$ dagi issiqlik:

$$Q_4 = I_4^2 R_4 t = 1^2 \cdot 12 \cdot 3 = 36 \text{ J}$$

Tok manbaida (ichki qarshilikda) ajraladigan issiqlik:

$$Q_r = I^2 r t = 4^2 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 = 96 \text{ J}$$

Butun zanjirda ajraladigan issiqlik:

$$Q_{umumiy} = I^2 R_{umumiy} t = 4^2 \cdot 10 \cdot 3 = 480 \text{ J}$$

Natijalar: 32; 36; 96; 480 — bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
