106-masala

To'liq zanjir uchun Om qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob E

Savol

Doimiy tok elektr zanjirining FIKi qaysi formulalarda to’g’ri aks ettirilgan ($I, U, \varepsilon, R, r$ – mos ravishda tok kuchi, kuchlanish, EYK, tashqi va ichki qarshiliklar):

  1. $\eta = \dfrac{R+r}{R}$. 2) $\eta = \dfrac{\varepsilon}{U}$. 3) $\eta = \dfrac{U}{\varepsilon}$. 4) $\eta = R\varepsilon$. 5) $\eta = \dfrac{R}{R+r}$. 6) $\eta = \dfrac{IU}{\varepsilon(R+r)}$.

A) 1, 3. B) 5, 6. C) 3, 4. D) 3, 6. E) 3, 5.

Yechim

Foydali ish koeffitsienti ta’rifi bo’yicha manba bergan to’liq quvvatga nisbatan tashqi zanjirda ajralgan foydali quvvat nisbati:

$$\eta = \frac{P_{foydali}}{P_{umumiy}} = \frac{UI}{\varepsilon I} = \frac{U}{\varepsilon}$$

Bu — 3-formula.

Yopiq zanjir uchun Om qonunidan $U=IR$ va $\varepsilon = I(R+r)$ ekanini bilib:

$$\eta = \frac{U}{\varepsilon} = \frac{IR}{I(R+r)} = \frac{R}{R+r}$$

Bu — 5-formula.

Qolgan formulalar (1, 2, 4, 6) o’lchov birliklari yoki matematik tuzilishi jihatidan noto’g’ri.

Demak, to’g’ri formulalar — 3 va 5.

Bu E javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: E
