Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

Qisqa tutashuv toki 8 A bo’lgan manbaga 12 Ω tashqi qarshilik ulanganda manbaning FIK i 75% ga teng bo’ldi. Foydali quvvatni (W) toping.

A) 72. B) 48. C) 36. D) 16. E) 12.

Yechim

Berilgan: $I_{qt} = 8$ A, $R = 12$ Ω, $\eta = 0{,}75$.

Qisqa tutashuv toki $I_{qt} = \dfrac{\varepsilon}{r}$, ya’ni $\varepsilon = I_{qt} \cdot r$.

FIK ta’rifidan:

$$\eta = \frac{R}{R+r} \Rightarrow 0{,}75 = \frac{12}{12+r}$$

$$12+r = \frac{12}{0{,}75} = 16 \Rightarrow r = 4 \text{ Ω}$$

EYKni topamiz:

$$\varepsilon = I_{qt} \cdot r = 8 \cdot 4 = 32 \text{ V}$$

Zanjirdagi tok kuchi:

$$I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R+r} = \frac{32}{16} = 2 \text{ A}$$

Foydali quvvat:

$$P_{foydali} = I^2 R = 2^2 \cdot 12 = 48 \text{ W}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
