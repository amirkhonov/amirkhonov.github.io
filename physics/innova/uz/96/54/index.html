54-masala

To'liq zanjir uchun Om qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

Ushbu zanjirda $R_1=6$ Ω, $R_2=3$ Ω, $r=0,5$ Ω, $\varepsilon=45$ V bo’lsa, ampermetrning ko’rsatishini toping (A).

A) 10. B) 5. C) 12. D) 4. E) 6.

Yechim

Rasmdan ko’rinadiki, $R_1$ va $R_2$ o’zaro parallel ulangan, ampermetr esa $R_2$ tarmog’idagi tokni o’lchaydi.

Parallel ulanishning umumiy qarshiligi:

$$R_{12} = \frac{R_1 R_2}{R_1+R_2} = \frac{6 \cdot 3}{9} = 2 \text{ Ω}$$

Umumiy tok kuchi:

$$I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R_{12}+r} = \frac{45}{2+0,5} = \frac{45}{2,5} = 18 \text{ A}$$

Parallel qismdagi kuchlanish:

$$U_{12} = I \cdot R_{12} = 18 \cdot 2 = 36 \text{ V}$$

$R_2$ orqali (ampermetr orqali) o’tayotgan tok:

$$I_2 = \frac{U_{12}}{R_2} = \frac{36}{3} = 12 \text{ A}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
