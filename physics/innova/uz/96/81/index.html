81-masala

To'liq zanjir uchun Om qonuni

Toʻgʻri javob B

Savol

$r=0$, $R_1=R_2=20$ Ω, $C=2$ μF, $\varepsilon=4$ V bo’lsa, kondensatorning o’ng qoplamasidagi zaryad necha Kulon?

A) 0. B) $+4\cdot10^{-6}$. C) $-4\cdot10^{-6}$. D) $+8\cdot10^{-6}$. E) $-8\cdot10^{-6}$.

Yechim

Rasmdan ko’rinadiki, $R_1$ va $R_2$ ketma-ket ulangan, kondensator $C$ esa $R_2$ ga parallel (ular orasidagi tugundan manbaning ikkinchi qutbiga tutashgan).

Kondensator orqali tok o’tmaydi, shuning uchun $R_1$ va $R_2$ orqali bir xil tok oqadi:

$$I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R_1+R_2+r} = \frac{4}{20+20+0} = \frac{4}{40} = 0,1 \text{ A}$$

Kondensatordagi kuchlanish $R_2$ dagi kuchlanishga teng:

$$U_C = I\cdot R_2 = 0,1\cdot 20 = 2 \text{ V}$$

Zaryad:

$$q = C\cdot U_C = 2\cdot 10^{-6}\cdot 2 = 4\cdot 10^{-6} \text{ Kl}$$

Kondensatorning o’ng qoplamasi $R_1$ va $R_2$ orasidagi tugunga ulangan bo’lib, tok manbaning shu tomondan kirib, $R_1$ orqali shu tugunga oqib kelayotgani uchun bu tugun (demak, o’ng qoplama) yuqori potensialga, ya’ni musbat zaryadga ega bo’ladi:

$$q_{o’ng} = +4\cdot 10^{-6} \text{ Kl}$$

Bu B javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: B
