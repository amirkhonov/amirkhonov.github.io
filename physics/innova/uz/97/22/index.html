22-masala

Tok manbalarini ketma-ket va parallel ulash

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

EYK lari bir xil va ichki qarshiliklari $r$ bo’lgan $n$ ta o’zgarmas tok manbalari tashqi $R$ qarshilikka oldin parallel, keyin ketma-ket ulandi. Bu ikki hol uchun tashqi zanjirdagi tok kuchlarining nisbati topilsin.

A) $n$. B) $\dfrac{nR}{R+r}$. C) $\dfrac{R+nr}{nR+r}$. D) $\dfrac{nr}{R+r}$. E) $\dfrac{R-nr}{nR-r}$.

Yechim

Berilgan: $n$ ta bir xil manba ($\varepsilon$, $r$), tashqi qarshilik $R$.

Parallel ulanganda tok kuchi:

$$I_{parallel} = \frac{\varepsilon}{\dfrac{r}{n}+R} = \frac{n\varepsilon}{r+nR}$$

Ketma-ket ulanganda tok kuchi:

$$I_{ketma-ket} = \frac{n\varepsilon}{nr+R}$$

Nisbatni tuzamiz:

$$\frac{I_{parallel}}{I_{ketma-ket}} = \frac{\dfrac{n\varepsilon}{r+nR}}{\dfrac{n\varepsilon}{nr+R}} = \frac{nr+R}{r+nR} = \frac{R+nr}{nR+r}$$

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
