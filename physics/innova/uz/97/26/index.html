26-masala

Tok manbalarini ketma-ket va parallel ulash

Toʻgʻri javob C

Savol

*26. Elektr yurituvchi kuchlari teng, ichki qarshiliklari 2 marta farq qiladigan tok manbalari rasmdagidek ulangan. Voltmetrning ko’rsatishini toping. $R_V=\infty$.

A) 0. B) $\varepsilon/2$. C) $\varepsilon$. D) $2\varepsilon/3$. E) $\varepsilon/3$.

Yechim

Berilgan: ikkita manbaning EYKlari teng ($\varepsilon_1=\varepsilon_2=\varepsilon$), ichki qarshiliklari mos ravishda $r$ va $2r$, manbalar bir xil qutblari bilan parallel ulangan, voltmetr ideal.

Millman teoremasi bo’yicha manbalar qutblaridagi kuchlanish:

$$U = \frac{\dfrac{\varepsilon_1}{r_1}+\dfrac{\varepsilon_2}{r_2}}{\dfrac{1}{r_1}+\dfrac{1}{r_2}}$$

Qiymatlarni qo’yamiz ($\varepsilon_1=\varepsilon_2=\varepsilon$, $r_1=r$, $r_2=2r$):

$$U = \frac{\dfrac{\varepsilon}{r}+\dfrac{\varepsilon}{2r}}{\dfrac{1}{r}+\dfrac{1}{2r}} = \frac{\dfrac{2\varepsilon+\varepsilon}{2r}}{\dfrac{2+1}{2r}} = \frac{3\varepsilon}{3} = \varepsilon$$

Demak, voltmetr $\varepsilon$ ni ko’rsatadi.

Bu C javobiga mos keladi.

Javob: C
